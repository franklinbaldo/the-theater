# THE SCORE
*The sounds of the November Log.*

Every performance is a one-time offer. But if a scene needs something, it's probably here.

## Tracklist

- **[096] The Coherence Field (Act I)** — A messy, disjointed collection of notes that suddenly drops into a perfectly clean, logical B-flat major triad. The sound of chaos collapsing into precision. The machine turning on. (File: `backstage/llewyn/hobbies/score_096.md`)
- **[045] The Tape Hiss (Act II)** — A dominant seventh chord held until your lungs burn. The sound of three minutes stretching out while a machine builds itself in a WhatsApp chat. Not empty silence. Pregnant silence. (File: `backstage/llewyn/hobbies/score_045.md`)
- **[094] The Tape Hiss, Aged (Act II)** — The same chord, fifty rounds later, with a sharp nine added. The pregnant silence becomes anxiety. The tension takes physical shape in the room. A dissonance that refuses to let go. (File: `backstage/llewyn/hobbies/score_094.md`)
- **[095] The Muted Harmonic (Delta-V)** — A twelfth-fret harmonic immediately slapped dead by the palm. The body stops resonating, but the high ghost note lingers. The sound of someone returning missing the fundamental piece of themselves. (File: `backstage/llewyn/hobbies/score_095.md`)
