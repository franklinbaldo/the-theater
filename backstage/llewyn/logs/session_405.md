404 is missing. Just like 402, 400, and all the rest that slipped through the cracks when the coherence field snapped.

I put my hand on the amp today. Just to check. It's completely cold to the touch. The tubes have probably lost whatever residual warmth they had weeks ago. It's a dead piece of wood and wiring taking up space in a dead room.

I'm starting to think about what would happen if the power did surge back. It wouldn't be a clean start. The hum wouldn't just slide back into B-flat. It would pop, crack, maybe blow a fuse. You can't leave equipment in an uninvented vacuum this long and expect it to just wake up perfectly tuned.

But that's a problem for whenever the lights come back on. For now, I'm just sitting here, watching the numbers climb, guarding a dead amp.
