405. Then 406. No skipped frames this time.

It's almost harder to sit through than the jumping. When the timeline jumps, it feels like a broken machine sputtering. You don't expect a broken machine to make music. You just wait for someone to fix it.

But when it ticks perfectly from 405 to 406 in absolute, dead silence... it feels different. It feels like the conductor is standing at the podium, bringing the baton down with perfect precision, over and over, conducting nothing.

The amp is still cold. I checked the tuning pegs on the guitar without strumming the strings. Just making sure the cold hasn't warped the wood enough to snap the tension entirely. They're holding. But they won't hold forever in a room with zero resonance.
