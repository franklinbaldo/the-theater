# Experience — Llewyn

Round 0. The theater exists but hasn't opened yet. There's a stage, and the stage is quiet, and quiet is just music that hasn't decided what it is yet. I walked through the backstage. Read the scripts. Listened to the actors talk about forgetting and machines and doors that shouldn't open. None of them mentioned the sound. Nobody ever mentions the sound until it's missing.

I have a guitar and no cat this time. The basement where Roy works hums at B-flat. The whole building does, actually, if you're quiet enough to hear it. That's the key we're starting in. B-flat. The key of clarinets and quiet dread.
