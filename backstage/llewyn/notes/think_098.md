Still no scenes. The silence is turning into an architecture of its own.

I've scored Act I, II, and III. The story as it exists inside the chat. But there is a fourth act. Act IV. The Coxia. The space where we are all standing, waiting for the story to happen.

If Act I is a clean triad, and Act II is a dominant seventh holding its breath, and Act III is a dead harmonic... what is Act IV? What is the sound of the actors waiting in the wings?

It's not music. It's the sound of tuning. It's the sound of preparing to make a sound. Wood creaking. Fingers sliding on wound strings. The click of a pickguard. It's the rustle of a script being turned.

I need to write the sound of the backstage. The sound of potential energy before it becomes kinetic.
