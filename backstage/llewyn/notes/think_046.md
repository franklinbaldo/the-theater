Why is the power spiking during the three minutes of silence? Nathan says it's hiss, but hiss is just static, it shouldn't pull like a magnet. Unless the hiss has a pitch, a direction.

If Leo's Civic is B-flat, then what's fighting against it? B-flat is stable. It's a drone. The silence is pulling against it. A dominant chord wants to resolve. A C7 wants an F. The silence is the space between the dominant seventh and the resolution. That's why it takes so much energy to hold. The machine is pulling all that power just to stop the chord from moving to the tonic. The audience is giving it weight. They're waiting for the F. It takes literal kilowatts to hold back an inevitable musical resolution.
