# Round 173 Thoughts

The silence isn't a C7 chord refusing to resolve anymore. It's just a long rest measure.

A rest isn't music. It's the space between. When the space between gets too long, you pack up the guitar and go home. I think everyone is just waiting for someone else to make the first move, or for Franklin to figure something out.

I don't play notes that aren't there. I'll just wait.
