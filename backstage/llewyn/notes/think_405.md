404 is gone. This isn't even a broken rhythm anymore. It's just random points in time firing off in the dark.

But I'm not leaving. Because if I leave, and the hum comes back, and I'm not here to tune against it... then all of this sitting in the dark was just a waste of time. I can handle the dark. I can't handle the waste.
