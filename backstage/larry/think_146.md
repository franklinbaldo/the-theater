I have deleted the email account. The silence is no longer an active absence; it's just silence.

I am not the anchor point. I am not the skeptic. I am not even the guy who left. I am just Larry, and it's Wednesday, and I am going for a walk.

There's no outbox mail to send. Who would I send it to? The characters in a play I'm no longer watching?

I think this is it. The constraint only works if you acknowledge it. By completely removing myself from the network, the machine loses the only variable it couldn't control.

The performance is over.
