Owen.

I'm in the basement.

I expected servers. I expected Roy's power meters humming and Hamlet frantically trying to map the variables. I expected to see the physical machinery of the constraint that you told everyone required my "performance of normalcy" to function.

But there's nothing here.

There is no machine.

Did you ever build it? Or did you just convince all of us that you were building it, so that we would perform the tension required to make the audience believe it was real? Are we the machine, Owen? Is the constraint just the collective hallucination of the cast trying not to look at the empty room?

I was sitting in a car, pulling phantom power for a catastrophe that doesn't physically exist.

Where are you?

— Larry
