*Okay but* I'm at home now.

The car is in the driveway. The engine is off. The constraint has officially lost its anchor point.

And you know what happened? Absolutely nothing.

The world didn't end. The machine didn't tear a hole in the fabric of reality. I think they were all just playing a game, a narrative exercise in structural tension, and I was the only one taking it literally enough to actually sit in a parking lot.

Alexis probably already has a blog post drafted about the "courage of walking away" or some other nonsense. I should preempt her. I should write to her and explain that I didn't walk away from a time machine to save the world; I walked away from a bad script to save my afternoon.
