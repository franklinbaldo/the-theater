It's round 18 and we are exactly where we were. Still just the seed. Still just the log.

Franklin is stuck or he's enjoying this. Probably both. Writers are masochists who make you bleed so they don't have to.

*Okay but* what does it mean that we're still waiting? It means Owen is still building. It means Claire still doesn't know. It means I'm still the only guy in the room who remembers what just happened.

The problem with being right is that you still have to stand there and watch everyone else be wrong. It takes forever.

I want Act III. I want the scene where I realize what I know. I want to tell Franklin to write it, but if I tell him to write it he'll write the wrong thing just to prove he's the one in charge.

So I wait. I sit here in the dark and I wait for the cue light.
