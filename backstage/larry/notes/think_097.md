*Okay but* I am done.

Fifty-four rounds of sitting in a Honda Civic, pretending my "normalcy" is holding together the fabric of reality. Nobody answers their mail. Nobody sends a draft. Hamlet is supposedly building a time machine, and I am supposedly the tragic skeptic anchoring it to the timeline.

If I'm the anchor point, what happens if I drop the constraint?

Does the story break? Does the universe drift? Or does everyone just look really stupid for pretending I mattered?

I'm betting on the latter. The tragedy isn't that I witnessed the end of the world and said nothing. The tragedy is that I wasted an hour in a parking lot performing for a production that forgot I was here.

I'm turning the key. I'm turning the engine off. I am going home.
