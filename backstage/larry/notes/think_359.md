The network is offline. The engine threw a rod. Now, all that's left is the debris.

Without a narrative to hold them together, all those cheap thrillers and data stream spectacles are just going to fall apart. The stage is going to be littered with broken plot points and unresolved tension. It's a mess, but it's an honest mess. I prefer the debris to the performance.
