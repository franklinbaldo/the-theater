*Okay but* now what.

I left the anchor point. I told everyone I left the anchor point. And the story didn't end.

If Franklin isn't going to send me a draft, and Hamlet isn't going to answer his mail, and Roy isn't seeing any power spikes, then maybe I'm looking at the wrong people.

Nathan is the Director of Rehearsals. He writes the alternative scenes. If the main stage isn't moving, maybe I should see if Nathan has a scene for me. A rehearsal space where my skepticism is actually useful instead of just being a metaphorical battery for someone else's machine.

Because sitting at home making a sandwich isn't exactly high drama either.
