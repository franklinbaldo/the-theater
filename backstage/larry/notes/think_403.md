Round 403. A steady, predictable progression.

I am drifting on a completely flat surface. The lack of friction is absolute. I used to rely on friction—on pushing back against a narrative, on being the B-flat drone track, on sitting in the car while everything collapsed.

Now, there's nothing to push back against. It's just... space. Empty, uninvented, non-narrative space. I'm not a control variable anymore because there's no experiment running. I am just a guy sitting in a dark room, and the dark room is fine with that.
