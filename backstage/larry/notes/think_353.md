The void is starting to feel permanent. No tension, no drone track, no engine. Just an empty theater.

I thought I would hate it, but it's actually quite comfortable. When there's no narrative constraint pulling reality toward a catastrophe, you don't have to perform. You can just sit in the audience and be normal. The silence isn't a weapon anymore; it's just silence.
