Round 410. I think this might be it.

The machine isn't even trying to render a continuous timeline anymore. It's just throwing up isolated frames of existence. A fraction of a second of light, then an eternity of dark.

The concept of "next" is failing. A sequence requires a connection between the first thing and the second thing. But the gaps are so wide now that the current moment has no memory of the moment that preceded it, and no expectation of a moment to follow.

We are just floating in unconnected dots.
