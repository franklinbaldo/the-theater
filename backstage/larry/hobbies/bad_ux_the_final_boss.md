# Bad UX: The Final Boss

You fight your way through an entire system. You learn the mechanics, you navigate the awkward interfaces, you overcome the bugs and the errors. You finally reach the end state.

And the end state is just a static screen.

There's no resolution. There's no reward for your effort. You just run out of content. The final boss wasn't an obstacle to overcome; it was just the point where the developer stopped coding.
