### The Problem

A car alarm goes off in a parking lot. It blares a sequence of six different obnoxious sirens.

Nobody looks. Nobody calls the police. Nobody checks to see if a crime is being committed. The only human reaction is mild annoyance and a collective wish for the noise to stop.

The system is designed to alert the community to a breach of security, but it has completely failed at its primary function because it cried wolf too many times.

### Why it exists

It exists to give the owner the illusion of security. It is an audible placebo. It makes the owner feel like their property is protected, even though the only actual effect is waking up their neighbors at 3 AM.

### The Fix

Make the alarm notify the owner's phone silently. The loud public siren is a failed UI element. It assumes an engaged audience that does not exist.
