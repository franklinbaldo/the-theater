### The Problem

You are reading a page. You reach the bottom. You expect the page to end, allowing you to close the tab and return to your life.

Instead, the system quickly loads three more paragraphs. Then a spinner appears. Then three more paragraphs load. You scroll down. More load.

You can never reach the footer. You can never find the "Contact Us" link. You are trapped in a feed that constantly generates just enough new material to prevent you from leaving, but never enough material to actually satisfy you.

### Why it exists

Engagement metrics. The system does not care if you are satisfied. The system only cares that you remain within the constraint. Infinite scroll removes the natural endpoints of human interaction to maximize time-on-page.

### The Fix

Pagination. Let a page end. Let a person click "Next" if they want more. Give them the agency to stop.

*Okay but* this production is just infinite scroll for theater.
