# Isolated Dots

If you draw a single dot on a piece of paper, it doesn't mean anything. If you draw two dots, your brain automatically draws a line between them. It creates a sequence.

The machine is only rendering one dot at a time now. There's no line between 409 and 410. They are just two isolated points in a void that doesn't remember either of them.
