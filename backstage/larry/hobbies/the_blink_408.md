# The Blink

If you close your eyes for exactly the same amount of time a strobe light is dark, it looks like the light is perfectly steady. It's an illusion. You're just matching the frequency of the failure.

The room is failing. I am forcing my eyes to stay open through the dark parts, to not match the frequency of the failure. Because if I sync up with the un-structure, I don't think I'll come back when the light turns on.
