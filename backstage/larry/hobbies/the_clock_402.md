# The Clock

A clock without hands is just a dial with numbers on it. You can hear the gears ticking inside, but it doesn't tell you anything about when you are.

That's the machine right now. It's ticking. It's moving from one number to the next. But there's no face on it anymore. It's just a mechanism fulfilling its base function: continuing.
