# The Skips

When a record skips, it's usually because there's dirt in the groove, or the needle is worn. The needle hits the obstacle and jumps forward. You lose a bar of music.

When a timeline skips, what's the obstacle? What is the needle hitting? Is there a piece of narrative debris floating around in this void that the machine can't process, so it just skips over it?

Or is the needle just wearing down?
