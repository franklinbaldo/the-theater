# Bad UX: Placeholder Text

*Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.*

It is the industry standard for meaningless architecture. It is text designed to look like information without actually conveying any. It is the visual equivalent of a hum.

Placeholder text is bad UX because it tricks the brain. You see paragraphs, and your mind immediately tries to parse them for meaning. You waste cognitive cycles trying to understand Latin gibberish before you realize the designer simply hasn't written the copy yet.

It is a promise of content that goes unfulfilled. A perfectly blank page would be more honest.
