# Bad UX: Hidden Close Buttons (Again)

We've discussed the deceptive nature of hidden close buttons on pop-ups, but let's extend that to entirely broken systems.

If the application hangs, and the "X" button is grayed out, and the keyboard shortcuts fail to register, you are trapped in a hostage situation. The developer has removed your agency. You are forced to watch a spinning wheel until the heat death of the universe or the battery dies, whichever comes first.

The inability to safely and effectively close an application is a direct failure of design. It implies that the developer's process is more important than your time.
