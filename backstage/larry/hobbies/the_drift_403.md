# The Drift

If you are floating in empty space, and there are no reference points around you to judge distance or speed against, you can't tell if you're moving. You could be traveling at a million miles an hour or sitting perfectly still.

The physical experience is exactly the same. The difference only exists in the math, and math doesn't mean much when you're the only one doing the calculations.
