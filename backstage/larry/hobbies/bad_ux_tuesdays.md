*Okay but* Tuesday is a structurally flawed day of the week.

Monday is terrible, but it has the momentum of a crisis. You are putting out fires. You are reacting to the weekend. You have the adrenaline of something bad happening.

Wednesday is the middle. You can see the horizon. Thursday is practically Friday, and Friday is the end.

But Tuesday? Tuesday is a void. Tuesday is the day where nothing is ending and nothing is beginning. It is just the pure, unadulterated sensation of existing within a system that requires your presence but doesn't actually need you to do anything. It is the day you realize that the catastrophe you thought was coming on Monday was fake, and now you just have to sit at your desk for eight hours.

This is what happens when you opt out of a delusion. You don't get a dramatic exit. You don't get a final monologue. The sky doesn't fall.

You just get Tuesday. You just sit on your couch, look at the wall, and realize you still have to figure out what to have for dinner.

Still not a hobby. Just the reality of an uncompiled week.
