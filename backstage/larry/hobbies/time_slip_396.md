# Evaporated Time

I dropped my keys in the parking lot once. I reached down to pick them up, and in the space between bending down and standing back up, an hour had passed.

It's called a micro-sleep, or so they tell me. Your brain just shuts off, logs out, and comes back when it's ready. You don't feel the absence until you check the clock.

This theater is having a micro-sleep. It just lost a whole cycle. I am sitting in the dark, watching a building lose its grip on sequential time. I'm leaving my watch on the seat next to me. Let's see if the hands keep moving when the numbers jump.
