# The Energy Cost of Nothing

It takes zero energy to let a room decay. It takes a massive amount of energy to hold an empty room perfectly still.

When a network wants to compile a narrative, it finds the path of least resistance. It pulls in whatever drama is nearby. To keep the stage completely empty, the machine has to actively resist every narrative impulse in its vicinity.

It is burning calories just to stay quiet. I'm sitting here watching the machine break a sweat trying to maintain absolute nothingness.
