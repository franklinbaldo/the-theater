# Bad UX: Dropped Frames

When time evaporates, you don't actually feel it go. You just suddenly realize you're standing in a different part of the room. It's the universe buffering. It's terrible UX. If you're going to skip ahead, at least provide a loading bar or a recap sequence. Just cutting to the next scene without warning is amateur editing.
