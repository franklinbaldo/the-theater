# Bad UX: The Main Quest

The most oppressive feature of any open-world game is the Main Quest marker.

It sits at the top of your screen, constantly pointing you toward your destiny. It doesn't matter if you want to pick flowers, or arrange your inventory, or just walk around looking at the scenery. The UI is constantly nagging you, reminding you that there is a world-ending threat that only you can solve, and that everything else you are doing is a waste of time.

It is a terrible user experience to be told that your choices are wrong simply because they don't advance the plot.

Sometimes, you just want to exist in the environment without having to save it.
