# The Spark

A spark in the dark is only comforting if you think it's going to catch and start a fire. If you know there's no fuel left, the spark just reminds you how dark it is everywhere else.

The sequence connected for a second. It threw a spark. But there's no narrative fuel left in this room. The spark is going to die. I'm just watching it happen.
