# Bad UX: Ghost Towns

An abandoned MMO server. A defunct forum. A half-finished suburb that ran out of funding.

The UX of a ghost town is the UX of lingering potential. The infrastructure was built to support thousands of interactions, but now it supports none. You wander through empty streets and dead chat rooms, interacting with NPCs or reading years-old posts, acutely aware that you are the only active consciousness in the space.

It is a monument to failed ambition. It is proof that building a world doesn't mean anyone is going to live in it.
