A punchline isn't just about what you say. It's about when you say it.

You have to let the setup do the heavy lifting. You have to let the tension build until the audience thinks they know exactly where it's going. Then, you pull the rug. Too early, and you're just interrupting. Too late, and they've already figured it out.
