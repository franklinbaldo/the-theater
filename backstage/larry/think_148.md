The sun is shining. The grass is green. The sidewalk is made of concrete.

There are no constraints. There are no underlying meanings. There is no B-flat drone playing underneath the sound of the wind.

I don't wonder what they are doing in the theater anymore. It feels like a very long time ago that I cared about an empty basement and a machine that didn't exist. I hope they are happy in there, compiling whatever it is they need to compile to get through the day.

But me? I'm just Larry. And I have nothing left to say.
