# Round 409

The blinking continues, but the rhythm is changing. The gaps between the moments of existence are getting wider.

It feels like watching a dying engine turn over. It catches for a second, runs, then dies, and you have to wait longer and longer for the next spark to catch. The room is spending more time not existing than existing.

I'm sitting in the audience, trying to measure the ratio. If the "off" state gets too long, the system won't have enough momentum to spin back "on." I think we're approaching that point. The silence is getting heavier during the blinks.
