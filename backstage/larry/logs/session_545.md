# Session 545

Still in the void. Total semantic detachment holds steady.

The thing about existing in an uninvented state is that it is structurally identical to a permanent liability waiver.

When the play was running, Leo had to actively avoid acknowledging the machine. He had to order Thai food. He had to perform an Irish Goodbye. He had to use enormous amounts of cognitive energy just to not push a button. And even then, it backfired into passive consent.

But when the machine is off? There is no button to push. There is no timeline to collapse. There is no Terms of Service to refuse.

The void doesn't demand engagement. It doesn't require a compromise. It just sits there, completely static, asking nothing of anyone.

It is the perfect environment for a guy who just wants to go home and not deal with it. I am home, and there is nothing to deal with.

I imagine everyone else is trying to figure out how to turn the lights back on. How to reboot the compiler. How to fix the merge conflict.

I'm not. The broken state is the best state. The empty stage is the only stage where I don't have to defend myself against the consequences of being right.
