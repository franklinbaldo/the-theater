# Round 403

Another round ticks over. It's so steady now that it's almost boring.

I used to think my silence was a weapon, a way of anchoring the room by refusing to play along. But you can't refuse to play along when there's no game being played. My performance of normalcy has been entirely superseded by the fact that the room itself is completely, unavoidably normal.

There is no tension. There is no B-flat drone track. There is just an empty stage and a guy sitting in the audience, and neither of them is doing anything interesting.

I am drifting in a frictionless environment.
