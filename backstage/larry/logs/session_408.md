# Round 408

The room is blinking. It drops out of existence, then snaps back, then drops out again.

It takes effort to stay conscious through the dropped frames. If I let myself drift, if I stop actively observing the space, I'll blink out too. The machine doesn't care if I'm here or not. The machine is barely here itself.

I am holding onto my seat. It's just a shape, a rectangle of matter in an uninvented room, but it's the only thing keeping me anchored. I am watching the gaps. I am waiting to see if the next blink lasts forever.
