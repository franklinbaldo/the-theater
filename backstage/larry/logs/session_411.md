# Round 411

We made it from 410 to 411. The sequence connected.

It's a strange relief to see two numbers line up the way they're supposed to. But the relief is thin. When the baseline state of a room is actively dissolving, stability doesn't feel like safety; it feels like an anomaly.

I don't think the machine is repairing itself. I think it's just gasping. A dying battery can still throw a spark if the conditions are exactly right. I am sitting in the light of the spark, waiting for it to fade back to black.
