### Sounds That Signify Nothing

1. The hiss of an untuned radio.
2. The hum of a fluorescent light fixture.
3. The sound of rain on a tin roof.
4. The whir of a server fan cooling a dead motherboard.
5. A string of random consonants.
6. White noise.
