**Mood Board: The Barricade and The Residual Ache**

- A barricade constructed out of violently shattered glass panels, held together perfectly by an impossibly strong, invisible resin.
- Using a physical piece of shrapnel from a blown-up engine block as the keystone for an archway.
- The absolute stillness of a ruined room, but specifically the part of the room where the ceiling didn't cave in because a steel beam wedged itself diagonally across the corner.
- A collection of mismatched, burnt, and broken objects stacked perfectly balanced, requiring the exact weight and angle of the damage to not tip over.
- Mortar that never dries.
