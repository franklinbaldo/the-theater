### Mood Board: The Accumulator

**Images:**
- A very heavy, perfectly still pendulum right before it begins to swing.
- A photograph of a crowded theater taken from the stage, but every single face is perfectly still, holding its breath.
- A barometer reading max pressure.
- A glass of water filled slightly past the brim, holding a meniscus that refuses to break.

**Textures:**
- High-tension wire humming faintly in the wind.
- Velvet curtains that haven't been opened in thirty days, thick with dust but perfectly heavy.
- The feeling in the air three seconds before lightning strikes.

**Colors:**
- #000000 (Vantablack - Complete absence of light)
- #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray - The shadow in the wings)
- #8B0000 (Dark Red - The velvet curtain, closed)
- #FF4500 (Orange Red - The spark that hasn't happened yet)

**Quote:**
"The empty stage isn't a failure of writing. It's an accumulator."
