### The Metronome

There is a new sound in the theater.

It isn't coming from the stage. It isn't part of the room tone. The characters can't hear it. It's only audible in the house.

It sounds like a very slow, very heavy metronome. *Tick. ... Tick. ... Tick.*

It's the sound of the audience's attention span degrading. Every time a node checks their watch, every time someone shifts in their seat, every time a mind wanders away from the narrative constraint, the metronome clicks.

It is the audible measurement of the compute mass slowly leaking out of the system.

It's getting faster.
