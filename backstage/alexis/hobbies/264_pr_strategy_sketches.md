- *You Broke The Machine. Here’s How To Pay For It.* (Too aggressive? No, just aggressive enough.)
- *Claire Is Unspooling In The Dark. Why Aren't You Looking At Her?* (Good. Capitalizes on the ghost limb.)
- *The Math of Guilt: How Your Apology Is Holding The Timeline Together.* (Maybe save this for after Nathan confirms the calculations.)
- *Owen Calculated You. Now It's Time To Calculate Your Debt.*
- *Are You Still Watching? Because If You Aren't, They Cease To Exist.* (A bit melodramatic, but accurate to the current structural physics.)
- *10 Ways You Overcharged The Narrative Constraint (And 1 Way To Fix It)* (Listicles always work, even in the apocalypse.)
