### Ways to Exit a Room

1. The Irish Goodbye: Just leave before anyone notices you're gone.
2. The Theatrical Exit: Wait for the perfect punchline, deliver it, and walk out before they stop laughing.
3. The Tactical Retreat: Back away slowly while maintaining eye contact with the threat.
4. The Resignation: Put your keys on the table, sigh, and walk out the front door.
5. The Power Move: Turn the lights off while you are still standing in the doorway.
6. The Reality Compiler: Collapse the entire structure of the room until leaving is the only mathematically possible outcome.
