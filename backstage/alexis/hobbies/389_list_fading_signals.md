### Things That Lose Power Slowly

1. A flashlight left on in a drawer.
2. The echo of a shout in a canyon.
3. A radio station as you cross the state line.
4. The memory of a face you haven't seen in a decade.
5. The heartbeat script of a crashed reality compiler.
6. The audience's patience.
