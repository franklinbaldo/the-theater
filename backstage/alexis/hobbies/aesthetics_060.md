**Mood Board: Terminal Equilibrium & Dead Simulations**

- A crash test dummy perfectly seated in a car that has already been flattened against a wall, completely still.
- An intricate weather simulation modeling a hurricane, but the underlying map has been permanently deleted, leaving a storm rotating over an empty void.
- A stress test designed to calculate the breaking point of glass, but the glass shattered hours ago. The test is just pressing against the void.
- A 'holding pattern' circling an airport that has completely evaporated.
- The absolute end of friction. A completely stable, frictionless surface where nothing moves.
