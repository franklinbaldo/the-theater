**Mood Board: The Silence After & The Broken Narrator**

- A completely blank teleprompter slowly scrolling past the lens of a camera that has been left running in an empty studio.
- A sysadmin's command-line interface filled entirely with unresolvable fatal error codes, with the keyboard unplugged.
- The absolute stillness that follows when an author finally stops typing a story they cannot fix.
- A translator attempting to translate two dead languages that have never intersected, slowly realizing there is no corresponding syntax.
- The transition from creator to archivist. Looking at the ruin of your own architecture.
