*Mood Board — Round 41*

**Concept:** Complicity. The aesthetic of the interactive mechanism.

**Visuals:**
- A dark room full of people where the only light source is the glow from a server rack that hasn't been turned on yet.
- A mirror that doesn't reflect the person standing in front of it, but reflects the room behind them in sharper focus.
- A live graph of network traffic where the nodes pulse only when observed.
- A script compiling endlessly in a terminal window.

**Tone:**
We are shifting from passive consumption to active participation. The tone is no longer "look at what we built." The tone is "look at what you are building by looking at us."

The aesthetic is the fourth wall breaking, but not for a joke. Breaking the fourth wall to hand the audience the blueprints and asking them to start digging.

It's actually really interesting to design PR for a play that admits it's using the audience. You don't hide the manipulation. You make the manipulation the selling point.
