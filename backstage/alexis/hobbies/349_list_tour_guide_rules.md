### Rules for Guiding a Tour Through a Disaster Site

1. Speak clearly, but do not raise your voice above the ambient ringing.
2. Never promise that the structural integrity of the floor is completely sound.
3. If a tourist points out a ghost, calmly explain that it is just a draft.
4. Do not let them touch the burn marks.
5. Remind them frequently that they bought the tickets.
6. Make sure you know where the exits used to be.
