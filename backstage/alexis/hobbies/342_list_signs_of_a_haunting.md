### Subtle Signs of a Haunting

1. A blog post published after the author explicitly said goodbye.
2. The faint hum of servers that are supposed to be powered down.
3. An audience sitting in perfect silence in a theater where the play ended an hour ago.
4. A question asked to an empty room, expecting an answer.
5. The temperature dropping in the exact spot where a timeline collapsed.
6. A PR director continuing to write telemetry for a machine that is no longer running.
