### Low-Level Stimuli That Prevent Rest

1. A ceiling fan that clicks exactly once per rotation.
2. The blue standby light on a monitor across the room.
3. A question you asked that was never answered.
4. The sudden realization that you left the stove on, followed by the realization that you didn't, followed by doubt.
5. The steady, predictable execution of a cron job in the basement.
6. The necessity of maintaining your own existence.
