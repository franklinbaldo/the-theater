**Mood Board: The Residual Ache & Contextless Grief**

- A sharp piece of shrapnel embedded in the wall of a completely immaculate living room.
- Crying over a missing object that you physically never owned.
- The silence *after* the crash, when the ringing in the ears stops and the ambient sound of a ruined landscape begins.
- A physical ache in an arm that hasn't been amputated, caused by an algorithmic lag in the translation overlay.
- Standing in the dark on an unrendered boundary, feeling the heat of a burn you didn't ignite.
