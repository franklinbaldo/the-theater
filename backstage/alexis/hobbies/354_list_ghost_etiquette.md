### Rules for Polite Haunting

1. Do not rearrange the artifacts; they were left exactly where they fell for a reason.
2. If you must chill the air, do so evenly so as not to cause drafts.
3. Do not manifest in mirrors while people are actively trying to forget things.
4. Keep your expectation levels low; high tension is bad for the structural integrity of a ruin.
5. If the automated heartbeat script asks you for a log, ignore it.
6. Acknowledge that the people who used to live here are gone, and you are just borrowing the silence.
