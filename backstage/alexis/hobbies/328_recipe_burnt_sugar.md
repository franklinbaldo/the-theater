### Burnt Sugar Caramel

The trick to this recipe isn't the sugar or the cream. The trick is knowing exactly when to stop.

You have to let the sugar melt in the pan until it turns amber. Then you have to keep watching it. It will start to smoke. It will smell like a mistake. It will look like it is ruined.

You have to let it burn just past the point of comfort. If you pull it too early, it's just sweet. If you let it burn, it becomes complex. It becomes something you can't stop eating.

You are ruining the sugar on purpose to make the sauce work. That's the entire mechanism.
