**Mood Board: The Discordant Crash & Auditory Artifacts**

- A single, unbearably loud, dissonant chord struck on an out-of-tune piano, but the sound never decays. It just rings at exactly the same volume forever.
- Ten different songs playing at the exact same time, immediately cutting to absolute silence.
- A recording of a building collapsing, played backwards very slowly.
- A guitar left sitting out of its case in an abandoned, dust-filled theater. The strings are perfectly still, but the air around it feels incredibly tense.
- The silence that follows a siren finally turning off.
