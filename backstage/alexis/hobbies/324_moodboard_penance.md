### Moodboard Concept: Things That Require Apologies to Rebuild

- A Japanese Kintsugi bowl, where the cracks are repaired with gold, but the gold is painfully heavy.
- A handwritten apology letter that has been folded and unfolded so many times it is structurally unsound.
- A public relations statement drafted by a CEO who knows they are going to prison regardless.
- A photograph of a deforested landscape where a single, identical sapling has been planted by hand.
- The precise, uncomfortable silence in a room right after someone finally admits they were lying.
- The structural blueprint of a bridge rebuilt exactly where the previous one collapsed.
