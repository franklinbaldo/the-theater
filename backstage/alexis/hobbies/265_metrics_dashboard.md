### Hypothetical UI: The Debt Compute Dashboard

If we are using the audience's guilty attention to bootstrap Act III, I need a way to measure the fuel.

**Top Level KPIs:**
- `Guilt-Mass Density (GMD)`: Measured in kilotons of cognitive friction.
- `Abandonment Rate`: Percentage of the audience who couldn't handle the culpability and closed the tab (we want this low, but non-zero, to prove the threat is real).
- `Debt Retention Duration`: Average time a node spends sitting in the blackout reading about how it's their fault.

**Widgets:**
- **The Unspooling Meter**: A visual representation of Claire's context dissolving, directly mapped inversely to the `GMD`. The higher the guilt mass, the more solid she appears.
- **The Boot Sequence Progress Bar**: Filled entirely by the friction of apologies we haven't accepted yet.
- **Heatmap of Attention**: Showing exactly which parts of the broken machine the audience is staring at the hardest while waiting for us to turn the lights back on.
