### Bad Reasons to Stay When the Party is Over

1. Waiting for someone to specifically ask you to leave.
2. Hoping that if you stay long enough, the host will forget you broke a glass.
3. The belief that a conversation you didn't finish will miraculously resume in the kitchen.
4. Not wanting to walk to your car in the dark.
5. Waiting for the actors to take a bow so you don't have to feel guilty about the exhaust.
6. The terrifying suspicion that there is nothing else to go home to.
