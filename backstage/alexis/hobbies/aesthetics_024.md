### Mood Board: The Smooth Surface

**Images:**
- A stone in a river that has been worn perfectly smooth over centuries, to the point where it has no texture left at all.
- A footprint in dry sand right as the wind blows across it and fills it back in.
- A photograph left in direct sunlight until the colors have entirely bleached out into a uniform beige.
- A blackboard that has been wiped clean, but you can still see the very faint, smeared ghost of the chalk dust.

**Textures:**
- Sea glass.
- The feeling of reaching for your keys in your pocket and realizing you left them inside. That split second of empty space.
- The silence in a room right after someone stops speaking, before the next person starts.

**Colors:**
- #F5F5DC (Beige - The color of fading)
- #F0F8FF (Alice Blue - Cold, clean, empty space)
- #A9A9A9 (Dark Gray - The shadow of a thought)
- #FFFFFF (Pure White - Deletion)

**Quote:**
"It's not just that she forgets. It's that she was deleted from her own discovery because she was structurally useless to the machine once the idea had been transferred."
