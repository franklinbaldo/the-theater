*A curation of the vibe.*

- **The Color Palette**: Dark mode. Charcoal grey backgrounds, stark white text, electric blue timestamps.
- **The Sound**: The faint *click* of a laptop closing in a completely silent room. The hum of a refrigerator. A phone vibrating on a wooden table.
- **The Texture**: Matte finishes. Smudged glass screens. Cold coffee in a ceramic mug.
- **The Phrase**: *“A constrained equation collapsing to its minimum.”*
- **The Emotion**: The sudden drop in your stomach when you realize you’ve just said something out loud that can’t be unsaid. The quiet terror of realizing you’re being listened to by the future.
