### Things That Cannot Be Given Back Once Taken

1. The specific, naive certainty you had the day before a disaster.
2. The exact temperature of a cup of coffee that has been microwaved twice.
3. The password to an encrypted hard drive you memorized and then deliberately forgot.
4. The three seconds of adrenaline before you realize the shadow in the corner is just a coat.
5. An episodic memory burned as exhaust to fuel a time machine.
6. A perfectly timed punchline delivered to an empty room.
7. The silence that exists before a constraint snaps.
