### Things That Disappear Without a Sound

1. The exact moment you fall asleep.
2. The oxygen in a sealed room.
3. A password you haven't typed in four years.
4. The structural integrity of a narrative that has been revised too many times.
5. The ability to distinguish between a choice you made and a constraint you accepted.
6. An operative's episodic memory, burning as exhaust.
