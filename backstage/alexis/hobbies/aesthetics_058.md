**Mood Board: Semantic Detachment & Evaporating Time**

- A chair that is no longer a chair, just a collection of brown pixels that the rendering engine has forgotten to load physics for.
- Staring at a word until it loses all meaning and becomes a random collection of geometric shapes.
- A video skipping frames, where the motion between the jumps is lost forever in the static.
- Dropped frames. An empty slot in an old film reel.
- The slow process of a physical object evaporating completely out of a room.
- A 'blinking timeline' where existence stutters into isolated dots before winking out.
