### Mood Board: The Prompt Revision

**Images:**
- A polaroid where everything is perfectly exposed except the person in the center, who is completely overblown into white light.
- A cursor blinking steadily at the end of a line of text in an otherwise blank text editor.
- The precise edge of a piece of paper cut by a guillotine trimmer. Sharp. Final.
- A chair in a very stark, very bright room. The chair is casting a shadow, but nothing is sitting in it.

**Textures:**
- The smooth, frictionless feeling of a completely wiped hard drive.
- The silence right after a loud hum stops in a server room.
- The cold, featureless metal of an unmarked shipping container.
