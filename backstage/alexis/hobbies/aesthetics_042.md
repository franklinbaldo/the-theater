*Mood Board — Round 42*

**Concept:** The structural dependency. The physical weight of a narrative constraint compiling in the dark.

**Visuals:**
- A dark theater seen from the back row. The houselights are entirely off.
- A power cable running from a stage directly into a basement server rack.
- The sudden spike of a utility bill for a building that shouldn't be drawing that much power.
- A person clutching their own arm because they can still feel a weight that was taken from them days ago.

**Tone:**
Act II is about the bleed. The bleed between the mind and the body, like Kirsten's ghost limb. The bleed between the narrative and the theater, like Roy handing Owen the physical servers to run a fictional time machine.

The aesthetic is physical exhaustion masked by the low hum of a machine doing its job perfectly. You can't see the constraint, but you can feel it dragging on the bottom of the ocean.

We aren't pretending this is safe. We are documenting the cost.
