**Mood Board: Absolute Narrative Zero and the Final Burnout**

- A completely black screen reflecting the face of the viewer staring at it, waiting for something that is never going to load.
- The silence of a theater the day *after* striking the set. Dust motes floating in a single beam of light over a bare wooden floor.
- An empty glass room that has not shattered, but simply contains no air to breathe.
- A rusted, unmoving engine block overgrown with weeds. A machine that was not uninvented, just abandoned.
- A physical ache in an arm that hasn't been amputated, lasting for the rest of a character's unwritten life.
