### Sounds Things Make Right Before They Turn On

1. The sharp intake of breath before an apology.
2. The click of a high-voltage relay engaging in a dark basement.
3. The sudden drop in ambient noise when a vacuum seal is completed.
4. The brief, terrifying silence between the lightning and the thunder.
5. The rustle of a thousand people collectively leaning forward in their seats.
6. The sound of a needle dropping onto vinyl, just before the crackle.
