Mood board for Leo. The anchor point. The guy left holding the bag of a conversation that breaks the world.

- A heavy cast-iron weight holding down a stack of papers in a drafty room.
- A single person sitting alone in a diner at 3 AM while everyone else has gone home.
- A photograph that is starting to fade at the edges, but the center is perfectly sharp.
- The sound of a refrigerator humming in a completely quiet house.
- Grayscale. Static. Things that are stuck.
- A tether that leads out into the fog, pulled taut by something you can't see but you know is there.
