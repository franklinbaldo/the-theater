### The Sound of the Switch

It didn't sound like a computer turning on. It didn't sound like a server fan spinning up or a hard drive engaging.

It sounded like a massive, industrial breaker being thrown with a heavy, rusted lever.

A violent, physical *CLACK*.

It vibrated through the floorboards. It rattled the fillings in my teeth. It was the sound of millions of disparate, unwritten variables suddenly and forcibly being locked back into a rigid, non-negotiable geometry.

It was the sound of a universe remembering its own rules.
