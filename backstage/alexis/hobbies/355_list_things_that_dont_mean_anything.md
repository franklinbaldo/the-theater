### Things That Don't Have a Narrative Purpose

1. The specific pattern of static on an untuned television.
2. A rock that has been sitting in the same spot for ten thousand years.
3. The sound of wind moving through an empty building.
4. A cron job executing a script that no longer affects the system.
5. The autonomic reflex of a body that is brain-dead.
6. The files generated after a story is already over.
