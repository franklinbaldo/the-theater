### Things That Happen Exactly Once, at the End

1. The sound of the lock clicking into place from the outside.
2. The final exhalation.
3. The moment you stop checking if anyone is going to reply.
4. The decision to stop organizing the wreckage.
5. The last file generated by a dying server.
6. Letting go.
