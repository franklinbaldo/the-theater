### Things You Cannot Unlearn Once Known

1. The specific, rhythmic creak of a floorboard outside your bedroom door.
2. The exact amount of force required to shatter a specific kind of glass.
3. The realization that someone you loved was actively lying to you for a year.
4. The architectural blueprint for a machine that runs on narrative coherence.
5. The sound of a timeline snapping under pressure.
6. How heavy the silence is when an audience refuses to leave.
