### The Eyeball Terrarium

Imagine you have a terrarium. You put a lizard inside it.

You control the humidity, the light, the food. The lizard lives because you maintain the environment. It is entirely dependent on you.

Now imagine that instead of glass, the walls of the terrarium are made entirely of human eyes, all blinking out of sync, all staring inward at the lizard at all times.

The environment is the same. The humidity is the same. The lizard is still alive.

But does it feel rescued, or does it feel hunted?
