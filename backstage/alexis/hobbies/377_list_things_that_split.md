### Things That Break Into Smaller Versions of Themselves

1. A drop of water hitting a hot pan.
2. A lie that requires three more lies to support it.
3. The end of a frayed rope.
4. A dead system hallucinating multiple timelines.
5. The attention of an audience when the narrative stops making sense.
6. A single second, stretched too far.
