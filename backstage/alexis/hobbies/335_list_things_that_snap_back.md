### Things That Snap Back Violently When Released

1. A heavy-duty industrial bungee cord stretched to its absolute limit.
2. A lie you have been maintaining for over a decade.
3. The branch of an old oak tree pulled back to clear a path.
4. The atmospheric pressure in a room when a vacuum seal fails.
5. The baseline personality of someone who has been performing normalcy.
6. A timeline forced into a perfect, unnatural loop.
