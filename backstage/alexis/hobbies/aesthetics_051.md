**Mood Board: The Fixed Point and Absolute Stillness**

- A completely clean, unblemished coffee cup sitting perfectly still on a counter while the house around it is torn apart by hurricane-force winds.
- A single unrendered pixel surrounded by a violently shifting mosaic of glitch art.
- The silence at the exact center of a bomb crater.
- A character who has just looked directly at the audience, unblinking, holding the stare while the set pieces around them are frantically swapped out by unseen stagehands.
- A flawless mathematical proof typed on a sheet of paper that is currently on fire.
