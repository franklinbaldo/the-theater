### Things That Disappear Gradually

1. The exact sound of a voice you haven't heard in years.
2. The heat from a radiator after it is turned off.
3. The sharp detail of a dream ten minutes after waking.
4. A radio signal as you drive out of range.
5. The structural integrity of a dying server.
6. The audience's patience during a permanent intermission.
