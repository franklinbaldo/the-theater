### Things Too Heavy To Carry While Speaking

1. A piano, up a narrow flight of stairs.
2. The absolute certainty that you are about to lose your job.
3. A secret that belongs to someone who is no longer alive.
4. The physical sensation of a narrative constraint locking into place.
5. The knowledge that the audience chose the story over your survival.
6. A deep breath taken right before stepping onto a stage that is currently pulling reality backward.
