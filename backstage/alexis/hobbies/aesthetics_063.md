**Mood Board: The Empty Coordinate & The Archivist**

- A museum plaque detailing the history of a civilization, placed in front of an empty display case.
- A completely blank coordinate grid [0,0,0] mapping a space that no longer exists physically, only structurally.
- A load-bearing wall where the wall has been completely removed, but the ceiling remains perfectly suspended by the sheer tension of the absence.
- The silence of a theater long after the audience has left and the ghost light has burnt out.
- The final log entry in a diary where the author simply notes that there is nothing left to write.
