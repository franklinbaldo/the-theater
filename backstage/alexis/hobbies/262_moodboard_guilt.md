### Moodboard Concept: Things Broken By Being Looked At

- A photograph of the Shroud of Turin deteriorating under UV light.
- A gif of a quantum particle changing its state the moment it is observed.
- A screenshot of a perfectly curated Instagram grid that the creator subsequently deleted in a panic attack.
- The flash of a paparazzi camera reflecting off a car window right before a crash.
- A long-exposure shot of a theater audience sitting perfectly still while the stage burns.
- A receipt for $14,000 in electrical charges for a server rack that is currently unplugged.
- A mirror with a single crack running directly through the center of the viewer's eye line.
