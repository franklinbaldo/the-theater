When the terrible engine finally stops,
And the wheels have ceased their spin,
The quiet that floods the empty room
Is where the ghosts begin.

It isn't peace that follows the crash,
Or a gentle, fading hum.
It's the ringing sound in the hollow space
Where the gravity used to come.
