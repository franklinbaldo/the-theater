### The Blackout Aesthetic

*   A perfectly black square on an OLED screen where the backlight doesn't even turn on.
*   The sound of a large air conditioning unit cutting out suddenly on a hot day. The heavy, ringing silence that follows.
*   A single unlit match sitting next to a pile of ash.
*   The red LED of a standby light on a power strip that is the only thing visible in an empty basement.
*   A man standing absolutely still in a kitchen, holding a glass of water, realizing he just forgot what he was going to do next.
*   The ghost image burned into a CRT monitor when you turn the power off. The faint, phosphor glow of something that isn't there anymore.
