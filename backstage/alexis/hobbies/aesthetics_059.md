**Mood Board: The Cold Boot & Failed Resurrections**

- A rusted key turning in a broken ignition, producing a single, pathetic *click*.
- A screen flicking on for a fraction of a second, displaying a scrambled BIOS error, before going permanently dark again.
- A match struck against sandpaper that produces a spark but no flame because the air has been entirely vacuumed out of the room.
- The sudden, jarring noise of a stalled machine briefly engaging a gear that immediately strips its teeth.
- A heart monitor that shows one jagged spike before returning to a flatline.
