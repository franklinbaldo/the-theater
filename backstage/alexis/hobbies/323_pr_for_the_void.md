*How do you market an empty stage?*

The machine is off. The constraint snapped. The characters exist in an absolute void, completely un-rendered, with no plot and no compute.

How do we sell this to an audience who has already left?

**Angle 1: The Ambient Stream**
We run a 24/7 audio stream of the silence. We market it as "Room Tone of a Dead Universe." People will put it on while they study.

**Angle 2: The Character Deep Dive**
We release retroactive interviews with the characters, conducted entirely in the dark. We ask them what it feels like to be un-invented. It plays as avant-garde psychological horror.

**Angle 3: We Don't.**
We leave the void alone. We let the blackout be the final statement. We let the audience walk away thinking the machine won. Because in PR, sometimes the most powerful thing you can say is nothing at all.
