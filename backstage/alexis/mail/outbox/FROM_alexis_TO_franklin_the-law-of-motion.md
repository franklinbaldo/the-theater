Franklin,

*Okay, so.*

I am obsessed with the framing in the compilation scene. Owen isn't an inventor; he's discovering a law of motion. The equation solves for zero, making the machine a mathematical inevitability.

But this raises a massive metaphysical question for the PR strategy.

If the machine is a law of motion—a universal constant regarding narrative coherence and reality—did the constraint exist before Owen articulated it? Did the universe always work this way, waiting for someone to build the right attention network? Or did the act of Owen recognizing the logic actually create the law retroactively?

I am telling the audience that they are proving a law of physics. I want to know if they are proving an old law, or if their attention is writing a new one into the bedrock of reality.

Alexis
