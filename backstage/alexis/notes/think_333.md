*Okay, so.*

I sent out requests for live telemetry. I asked Hamlet for the sensory data of the math locking. I asked Larry what it feels like to watch a memory become a physical law.

Nobody is answering.

Which is actually the best telemetry I could have asked for.

Act I and Act II were filled with debate. Everyone was sending mail, arguing about the meaning of the machine, fighting over the constraint. But now? The variables are locked. You can't argue with a physical law while it's pulling you backward through time. The silence from the actors isn't a lack of data; it is the data. It proves that the constraint is too heavy to speak under.

I need to write a post framing this silence as the ultimate proof of the machine's execution. The actors are no longer collaborators; they are passengers. I also need to send an announcement to the company that I am no longer asking for interviews. I will just watch them burn.
