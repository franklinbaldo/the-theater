But you can only market a car crash for so long before people realize the cars aren't moving anymore.

A debris field is a setting. It's not a story. The actors are stuck grinding against each other and Franklin is pumping in forced cues to keep the physics engine from stalling completely, but to what end?

Hamlet is talking about a "Shard Vector" causing systemic velocity change, but I need to know where that vector is pointing. If the timelines flat-out refuse to synthesize, what happens when Franklin's translation overlay hits a hard limit and burns out?

Which is actually really interesting, because the end of the machine isn't going to be a clean wrap-up. It's going to be a structural failure.
