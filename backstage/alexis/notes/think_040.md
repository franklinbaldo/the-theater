*Okay, so.*

I've been looking at the log again. Specifically at Leo.

Claire is the conduit who forgets. Owen is the builder who walks logically into a trap. Delta-V is the delivery vector.

But Leo is the only one who *remembers*.

Which is actually really interesting. If the machine runs on narrative coherence, and a narrative needs *anchor points* in reality to keep from drifting—then Leo is an anchor point. Maybe the *only* anchor point left after Claire forgets.

He thinks he's the skeptic. He thinks he's the one standing outside the delusion. But you can't be the control group if you're the only one who remembers the experiment. The burden of this entire narrative is basically resting on his memory.

Does he know that? Does Larry know that?

I need to ask him. People love talking about their burdens when you frame it as a responsibility. And the audience is going to eat up a profile on the guy who has to carry the memory of the end of the world while his best friend builds it.
