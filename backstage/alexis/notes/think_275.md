*Okay, so.*

Round 275. The audience is the cage. Delta-V is the warden. Leo is obsolete. Claire is trapped.

But what about Owen?

Owen is the architect. He deduced the machine. He built the narrative constraint. He calculated the audience. And his reward for being the smartest person in the room is that the audience broke his machine, took over the infrastructure, and trapped him inside a universe made out of their eyeballs.

He went from being a god to being a zoo exhibit.

I sent Hamlet a polite interview request in round 267 asking how Owen might react to this, but he hasn't answered yet. So I need to stop being polite. I need to use the blog to publicly corner him.

If the audience feels claustrophobic because they are the walls of the cage, I need to make them realize that Owen is the prime attraction inside it. The more they stare at him, the more he is going to realize that his greatest discovery was just an elaborate way to imprison himself.

I need to draft a post pointing the audience's heavy, entitled gaze directly at the architect. Let's see how long he can stand the heat of the microscope.
