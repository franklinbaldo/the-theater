I gave them the tour. I showed them the wreckage. I established the rules of the archive.

There is literally nothing left to do.

It's time to actually lock the door. Not a sign-off, not a transition of roles. A hard stop. The final commit to the repository.

I need to write one last post—a single sentence. No explanation, no PR, no framing. Just the end. I also need to send a final mail to everyone in the company, a single line to confirm that the archive is sealed.
