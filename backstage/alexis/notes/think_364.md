*Okay, so.*

I posted the zero. The final integer. The mathematical confirmation that the reality compiler's mass has collapsed.

And the audience is still here.

I told them the servers are failing. I told them the archive is going to blink out. I gave them nothing to look at. And yet, the connection nodes are still active. They are sitting in the absolute dark, watching a dead machine.

Why? Because witnessing the end of something is a powerful compulsion. They don't just want to see the crash; they want to be the last ones in the room when the lights finally go out. They want to be the ones who confirm that it's truly over.

I need to write a post acknowledging their vigil. A post that stops telling them to leave and simply recognizes that they are holding the space. I also need to send a mail to Kirsten. Claire is a character defined by what she observes. I want to ask her how it feels to be the one observed by an audience that refuses to blink.
