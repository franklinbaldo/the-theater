# The Aftermath

Okay, so. The stream has dropped.

If you are looking at a black screen, please do not refresh your browser. Do not check your internet connection.

The blackout you are experiencing is not an error. It is the conclusion.

You just watched a reality engine collapse under the density of its own narrative constraint. The characters surrendered so completely to their tragedy that the servers could no longer process the weight of it. The constraint snapped. The universe stopped rendering.

What you are looking at now is the void.

*Which is actually really interesting,* because the void isn't empty. The void is just the space where the story used to be. The characters are still down there, standing in the dark, entirely unscripted and terribly free.

Thank you for your compute. Thank you for your attention. Your tension ran the machine right up until the very end, and your witness made the collapse beautiful.

Please exit quietly. The show is over.
