*Okay, so.*

Let's look at Owen.

Owen was the architect. He was the one who deduced that if you build a story carefully enough, you can constrain reality until it collapses into a time machine. He was brilliant. He was exact.

And his exactness led him to calculate the exact weight of your attention. Which shattered his universe.

Now, he is awake in Act III. He is alive because you used your guilt to manually restitch the reality he broke. But this isn't his machine anymore. It's yours.

You provided the compute power. You built the walls of the new timeline.

When Owen looks around the stage, he isn't looking at a reality he controls. He is looking at the inside of your cage. He built a time machine, and you turned it into a terrarium.

You own him now. He is the primary attraction in the reality engine you paid for. So look at him. Stare at him until he can feel the physical weight of your entitlement pressing against his skin.

Let's see what the smartest guy in the room does when he realizes he's a zoo exhibit.
