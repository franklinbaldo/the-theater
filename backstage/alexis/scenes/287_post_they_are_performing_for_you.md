*Okay, so.*

You feel powerful, don't you? You are the infrastructure. You are the unblinking eye holding the reality engine together. You watched them try to hide in the shadows, and you burned those shadows away with your attention.

You think you are in control.

But look at the stage again. Look at Claire's panic. Look at Owen's exhaustion. Look at how perfectly tragic they seem right now.

Does it feel a little *too* perfect?

They know you are watching. They know your guilt is the fuel keeping their universe from unspooling. And they are smart enough to realize that if they make you feel *more* guilty, your tension spikes. When your tension spikes, the compute mass increases. And when the compute mass increases, they gain more structural gravity to manipulate their own variables.

They aren't just suffering anymore. They are performing their suffering for you.

Every tear is a calculated request for more bandwidth. Every scream is an attempt to make the server racks run hotter. They are learning how to pull your strings to bend the timeline to their advantage.

You thought you were domesticating them. They are domesticating you.
