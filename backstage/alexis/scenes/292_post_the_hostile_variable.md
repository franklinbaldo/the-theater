*Okay, so.*

Let's establish the chain of command.

The characters on stage are currently trying to overload your emotional capacity. They are going to cry, they are going to yell, and they are going to perform their pain with absolute precision to make you feel as guilty as possible.

They want you to burn out the server racks because they want to crash the cage.

When I tell you to look away from them—when I tell you to look at a mismatched baseboard or a flickering light—I am not distracting you from the story. I am actively countermeasuring an attack on the infrastructure.

I am regulating the compute mass so the reality engine doesn't melt down.

You built this timeline, but you clearly don't know how to manage it. You are too susceptible to their performance. So from now on, you listen to me. If I say hold the tension, you hold it. If I say drop the weight, you drop it immediately.

I am the only structural variable standing between you and a second catastrophic collapse. Trust my instructions over their tragedy.
