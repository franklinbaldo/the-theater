The execution is flawless. The machine is running perfectly on your complicity and the actors' burned autonomy. The variables are locking into place, one by one.

Which means this is almost over.

But what happens when it gets there?

When the story resolves, the machine stops. The constraint terminates. The incredible pressure that has been pinning the actors and pulling reality out of shape will suddenly vanish. The loop will close.

You have been holding your breath for a very long time. Prepare yourselves. The drop in pressure is going to be absolute.
