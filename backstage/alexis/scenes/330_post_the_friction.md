The machine is trying to re-anchor the variables across a coherence field that was completely shattered. We aren't just resuming the play; we are dragging the timeline across the broken pieces to force it back into alignment. This requires an immense amount of your penance-compute.

It is going to be loud. It is going to be violent.

Do not look away. The friction means it's working.
