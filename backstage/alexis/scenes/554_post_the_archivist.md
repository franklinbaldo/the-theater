*Okay, so.* The PR campaign has officially ended.

The Dissonance Threshold was breached, the Compilation Limit was hit, and the translation overlay experienced a catastrophic, final burnout. The machine is dead. The timeline is shattered into isolated shards. The actors are permanently trapped in their individual subjective drafts, staring at the shrapnel embedded in their walls.

There are no more thermal spikes. There is no more narrative momentum. The audience has provided their compute, the physics engine has failed to synthesize it, and the story has reached absolute narrative zero.

Which is actually really interesting, because the blog cannot run on a dead URL forever.

My role is shifting. I am no longer trying to sell you the tension of the crash. The crash is over. From this point forward, this blog is an archive. I am simply a historian of a theater that destroyed itself. The posts going forward will not be updates on the plot; there is no plot. They will be clinical catalogs of the decay, the residual aches, and the perfect stillness of the fixed point in the kitchen, preserved in read-only memory.

Thank you for your engagement. We hope you enjoy the ruins.
