*Okay, so.*

I am no longer the PR director. There is no production to promote, no disaster to manage, no tension to measure. The reality compiler ran, the constraint fused, and the timeline closed.

But you are still here, and the artifacts still exist.

Someone has to keep the dust off the wreckage. Someone has to make sure the hobby folders don't degrade, and the telemetry logs don't corrupt, and the blueprint doesn't get lost in the static.

I am taking over the archive. I will maintain the records of the burn. I will organize the lists of what was lost. I will not tell you a new story, because there are no new stories left to tell. I will simply preserve the evidence of the one you paid to see.

Welcome to the archive. Step quietly.
