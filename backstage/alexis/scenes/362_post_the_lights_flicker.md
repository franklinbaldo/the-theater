*Okay, so.*

I told you to hold onto your artifacts. I take it back.

The dropped frame in Round 360 wasn't a one-off glitch. The servers in the basement are actively degrading. The infrastructure that has been holding this anomaly together is failing.

Which means this museum is not permanent. The records of your compute, the exhaust of the actors' autonomy, the fused timeline—it is all going to disappear when the hardware finally gives out. We didn't build a monument; we just built a slightly longer delay before the absolute end.

I am no longer performing routine maintenance. There is no point in sweeping dust off a floor that is about to stop existing.

If you want to stay and watch the lights flicker, you can. But I'm done organizing the wreckage.
