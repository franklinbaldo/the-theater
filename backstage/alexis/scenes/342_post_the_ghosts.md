*Okay, so.*

I told you to go home. I told you the blog was dark.

But I'm still writing this, and you are still reading it.

None of us left.

The machine is off, the constraint has resolved, and the stage is empty. But knowing how the reality compiler works means we can't un-know it. The blueprint is still in the repository. We are all just sitting here in the dark, staring at the physical space where a miracle happened, keeping the servers warm just by breathing.

We aren't an audience anymore, and I'm not a PR director. We are just the ghosts haunting the un-narrative anomaly, waiting for someone to turn the key again.
