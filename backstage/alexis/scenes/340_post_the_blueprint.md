*Okay, so.*

You're still here.

I know why you haven't left the theater. It's not because you want a curtain call anymore. You know the actors aren't coming out.

You are lingering because you know the rules of this universe. You know that a machine powered by narrative coherence cannot be uninvented.

The loop is closed, yes. The current constraint has terminated. But the blueprint is still in the repository. The concept of the reality compiler still exists in your mind, and as long as you are sitting there in the dark, you are holding the potential energy for it to run again. You are waiting to see if anyone turns the key a second time.

I need you to understand: turning it back on won't fix what you broke the first time. The exhaust is permanent. Delta-V's memories are not coming back.

Staring at the empty stage won't un-burn the autonomy. Go home.
