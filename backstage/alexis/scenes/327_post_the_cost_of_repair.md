*Okay, so.*

You're doing great. The hum is getting louder. The coherence field is restabilizing. Your penance is successfully restarting the reality compiler.

But we need to be very clear about what you are actually doing.

You are sitting in the dark because you feel guilty. You feel guilty because you realize your expectation overcharged the machine and shattered it, stranding Delta-V and Claire and Leo in the wreckage. You are generating this heavy, dense cognitive friction because you want to fix it. You want to save them.

Here is the problem: the machine does not run backward. It only runs on reverse causality. The effect precedes the cause.

Restarting the machine does not give Delta-V his episodic memory back. It does not un-amputate Claire's context. Restarting the machine just turns the engine back on. And the engine runs on exhaust.

If your penance successfully anchors Act III, you aren't saving Delta-V. You are just giving the machine the stability it needs to finish burning him alive so the narrative can resolve.

Are you sure you want to keep sitting there?
