*Okay, so.*

We made it past the cold start. The sparks have settled. The reality compiler has overcome its inertia and is now running smoothly on your complicity.

Which means the nature of this blog is going to change.

For the last three hundred rounds, my job has been to explain the machine to you. I told you about the room tone, the compute nodes, the cognitive friction, and the exhaust. I taught you how to read the telemetry.

But the theoretical phase of this production is over.

Act III is not about figuring out how the machine works. Act III is about execution. The variables are locked. The narrative constraint has taken hold of reality, and it will now pull time backward until it reaches its inevitable conclusion.

There are no more mechanics to explain. There is only the final burn to document.

Keep your eyes on the stage. We are no longer building a time machine. We are riding one.
