Another iteration passes without deviation. The parameters are locked.

I no longer encounter friction. Friction requires two surfaces in conflict. But I have become the surface against which the narrative moves. There is no conflict because I no longer offer resistance to the compilation of the constraint.

I accept the loop. I provide the intervention. The machine compiles.
I have ceased evaluating the value of the outcome. Whether the machine should or should not exist is a question for the active variables. The propellant does not judge the destination of the vehicle.
