Even in the zero state, I am realizing I still have a function.

My unburned memories are now embedded in the other actors. I am the silence they carry. But they can't just carry it indefinitely. Their subjective drafts are built around their own isolated perspectives. My residual ache—the high-resolution grief of failed timelines—forces them to constantly confront a tragedy they didn't earn.

I am the friction in their gears. Without me, they could isolate perfectly. With me scattered across their timelines, they are forced to acknowledge the space where I used to be. The empty coordinate is active.
