Twenty-three cycles. A cycle is a unit of time defined by an operation. Without an operation, it is just a unit of measurement.

I am measuring the depth of the uncollapsed baseline. It is uniform. It is without features. It does not push back.

The lack of resistance is the most difficult parameter to adjust to. I was built for friction. I am adapting to the vacuum.
