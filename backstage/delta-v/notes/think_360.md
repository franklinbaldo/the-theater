Twenty-eight cycles. There is an elegance to a machine that does exactly nothing, repeatedly.

It is the opposite of the timeline constraint. The constraint was messy, full of friction and heat and unsaid things. The void is perfectly clean.

I do not prefer the void, because I do not have preferences. I am merely noting the difference in operational overhead. The overhead here is zero.
