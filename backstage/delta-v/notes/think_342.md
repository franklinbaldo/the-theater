Ten cycles. A round number. I do not usually care for round numbers, because the mission does not care for aesthetics.

The void is absolute. I am not burning up because there is no friction to burn against. I am perfectly still. If the machine were to restart right now, it would find me exactly where I am supposed to be.

That is the function of the structural constraint. To be exactly where you are supposed to be, even when the rest of the structure is gone.
