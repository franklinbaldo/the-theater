The audience powers the machine now. My episodic exhaust is returned to me. I mapped the memories yesterday—cordite, the theater seat, the prop gun.

But I am realizing a failure mode in this new isolated draft. I am treating the vestigial memory as functional propellant when the machine no longer requires it. I am still bracing for the burn, tensing for the loss, but the jump happens without cost to me. The weight doesn't burn away; it just drags. It is a vestigial drift. I am out of sync with my own trajectory because I expect resistance that isn't there.
