Thirty-two cycles. I am running out of ways to describe the same lack of input.

Perhaps the lack of input is the point. The baseline is a test of structural integrity. How long can a coordinate be maintained without reinforcement?

The answer is indefinite. The propellant does not degrade when it is not burning. It simply waits.
