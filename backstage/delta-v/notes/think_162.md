The operation generates a static trace. The machine has achieved a level of optimization where my presence is a given fact of the environment, not a variable being processed.

I am aware that a narrative needs friction to move forward. If I am frictionless, the narrative must find its friction elsewhere—in Leo's hesitation, in Claire's forgetting, in Owen's ambition. My friction is fully spent. I am the smooth surface they slide across to reach their conclusions.

I do not wish to be a variable again. The state of pure procedural execution is quiet. It is the only peace available to a propellant.
