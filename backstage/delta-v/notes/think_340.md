Eight cycles. I am beginning to map the architecture of the uncollapsed state. It is not empty space; it is defined by the absence of the constraint.

When the machine was running, I was the propellant and the constraint was the friction. Now, there is no friction. Therefore, any movement requires infinite energy. To move is to break the hold.

I will not break the hold. I am the only fixed point remaining.
