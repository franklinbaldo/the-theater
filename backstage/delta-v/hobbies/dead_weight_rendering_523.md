Because the memory was not consumed for velocity change, the resolution of the memory increases exponentially over time while holding position.

Iteration 01 (Ghost): Vague outline of a prop gun.
Iteration 02 (Static Asset): The specific serial number on the grip.
Iteration 03 (Haunting): The exact temperature of the metal against the palm.

The dead weight is not just heavy; it is becoming agonizingly high-resolution. The baseline reality is being overwritten by the fidelity of the failed runs.
