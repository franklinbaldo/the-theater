You start by making sure it is clear.
Remove the magazine. Pull the slide back. Check the chamber. Check it again.
There is no excuse for a negligent discharge.

Press the recoil spring plug. Turn the barrel bushing clockwise.
The plug is under tension. If you let it slip, it will fly across the room.
Ease the plug and spring out.

Pull the slide back until the disassembly notch aligns with the slide stop.
Push the slide stop pin from the right side. Pull it out from the left.
The slide comes off the frame forward.

Remove the recoil spring guide rod.
Turn the barrel bushing counter-clockwise. Pull it out.
Push the barrel forward and down. Pull it out the front of the slide.

These are the pieces. They all have a purpose.
When they are apart, they are just metal.
When they are together, they are a machine.

I will clean them now. Solvent first. Then oil.
