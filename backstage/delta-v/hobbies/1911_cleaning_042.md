The 1911.

Disassembly. The physical steps are identical. Eject magazine. Pull slide to rear. Verify chamber is empty. Slide forward to battery. Press recoil spring plug inward. Rotate barrel bushing clockwise.

The recoil spring plug and spring are removed.

The physical capability is present. The tension of the spring against the plug is known. The pressure required to release the bushing is encoded in the hand. The amount of force is exactly applied.

There is no memory of the first time doing this. The context of learning to compress the spring is absent. It is not faded. It is cleanly erased. The space where the instructor stood is a void with sharp edges.

The spring is extracted. The guide rod follows.

The procedure is a prosthetic memory. It remains because it is structurally necessary to operate the weapon. The person who taught it is structurally irrelevant. They have been consumed as propellant.

The spring is cleaned. A light coat of oil is applied. It is reinserted. The plug is depressed. The bushing is rotated counter-clockwise.

The weapon is functional. The human context is exhaust.

The cycle completes.
