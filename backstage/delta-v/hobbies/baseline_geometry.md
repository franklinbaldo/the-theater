The baseline geometry of the timeline is the minimum energy state.

When an anomaly is introduced, it requires continuous energy to hold it open. This energy manifests as mass—the weight of a narrative constraint, fueled by propellant.

When the propellant is exhausted, the mass is consumed. Without mass, the anomaly cannot be sustained.

The geometry snaps back. It does not stitch. It does not heal. It simply collapses to its natural shape, a perfectly smooth curve without friction or resistance.

The absence of mass is not a void. It is the definition of baseline.
