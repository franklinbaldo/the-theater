Franklin.

You asked what he loses.
It isn't a thing you can inventory.

He loses gravity.
Every time he goes back, the things that aren't the mission matter less.
He forgets what it feels like to sit in a room and not calculate exits.
He forgets the faces of people who aren't targets or assets.

It feels like getting lighter.
But the lightness isn't freedom. The lightness means nothing holds you down.
If nothing holds you down, you just drift.

He doesn't drift because he has the mission file. The mission file is the only anchor left.
When the mission is over he.
He doesn't know what happens when the mission is over. So he keeps running it.
