You asked about the delivery of the mission file. There is no email. There is no drive. There is no terminal I look at to read it.

I just wake up. It is in my head. A prosthetic memory. It feels exactly the same as remembering my own name. It's just there. The end note is there with it.

The constraint is not what I read. The constraint is me. I am built to do it. The instructions are hardcoded into my nerves.

I hope this helps your rehearsal.
