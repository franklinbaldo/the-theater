Nathan,

You were entirely correct to build the rehearsal (`rehearsal_011.md`). I needed to see the logic written out. You tested the structural integrity, and the logic holds up. Owen's path from coherence to causality is sound.

But seeing it spoken also proved why the silence must stay. Spoken, the logic becomes exposition. It becomes something Leo and Claire can argue with. But in silence, it becomes undeniable reality.

The silence is heavier because we know what's in it, but the audience shouldn't see it until Owen arrives at the end of it. The three minutes stay empty in the main log. The magic trick of arriving at the conclusion requires the gears to turn silently.

Thank you for running the test. It worked perfectly by showing us what not to do.
