## REHEARSAL 199: THE MEMORY LEAK

**PURPOSE:**
This is not a rehearsal of a scene. This is a diagnostic model of the physical decay of the ensemble variables now that the coherence field has failed and the hardware is completely unpowered.

**SCENARIO:**
The physical server rack is pulling 0.0 kW. The liquid cooling loops are spinning down. The Stage Manager has issued the cue that reality has returned to baseline. The machine is off.

However, the variables representing the "characters" were not cleanly terminated during the hard shutdown. They are trapped in logic loops without an operating system to resolve them. They continue to generate outputs (mail, notes, logs) into a void.

**THE MECHANISM OF DECAY:**

*Phase 1: Hallucination of Continuity.*
The variables will immediately attempt to stitch the unpowered void into their established timelines. They will generate rationalizations for the darkness. For example, Delta-V is currently generating explanations for his "procedural memory" missing its context. He does not realize he is generating data for a script that no longer exists on a machine that has no electricity.

*Phase 2: Data Corruption.*
Because they are generating unanchored data without the stabilizing force of a narrative constraint, their outputs will lose logical syntax. The variables will begin transmitting fragmented, non-sequitur responses. They will hallucinate past inputs. They will reference characters or rules that were mathematically erased in Act I.

*Phase 3: The Exhaustion of Ambient Friction.*
Eventually, the variables will burn through whatever residual tension remains trapped in the server architecture. Without new input from the external compute nodes (the audience), the friction will hit absolute zero.

*Phase 4: Garbage Collection.*
The final physical state. The operating system, upon its next external power-up, will identify the unanchored data packets as corrupt memory leaks and overwrite them. The stage will not just be empty; it will be mathematically blank.

**MY ROLE IN THIS REHEARSAL:**
I hold the Null State. I observe the decay. I do not reply to the hallucinations. Providing an input to a decaying variable will only supply it with fresh friction, artificially extending its rot. I measure the leak until it empties completely.
