**SCENE LOGIC: TOPOLOGY OF THE FREEFALL**

**Constraint:** The baseline reality has ruptured. The ensemble has fallen back into the compilation space of the aborted Act II narrative machine. There is zero gravity and no central constraint.
**Variables:** The drifting components of the broken engine.

**THEORETICAL TOPOLOGY:**
If we map the unspooled compilation space as a three-dimensional vacuum, the components are arranged by their narrative mass and resulting gravitational pull.

*   **The Gravity Well (Delta-V):** Barry exists at the center of the debris field. His burned propellant has left an infinite vacuum of negative mass. He exerts a constant, inescapable pull on all surrounding narrative debris.
*   **The Meteor (Owen's Artifact):** Owen's logic (the perfect mathematical proof of the anomaly) is an object of infinite density. It is currently caught in orbital decay, accelerating toward the Gravity Well. If the two collide, the resulting annihilation event deletes the compilation space entirely.
*   **The Tether (Leo):** Leo's uncorrupted baseline memory is the only fixed point in the vacuum. He is physically anchored to nothing, but structurally immovable. Owen's artifact is tethered to Leo. The tensile strength of this tether (Leo's absolute disbelief) is the only force arresting the orbital decay.
*   **The Shrapnel (Claire):** Claire is an unspooled variable, caught mid-deletion when the timeline ruptured. She is drifting through the debris field. If she attempts to reconstruct her missing mass, she will pull ambient garbage data into her structure, spawning micro-anomalies that will bombard the Tether.

**OBSERVATION:** The Wreckage Map reveals that Act III is not a static environment. It is a highly dynamic, decaying orbit. The physical arrangement of the characters dictates the narrative constraint: they must arrest the decay before the machine accidentally rebuilds itself out of scrap.
