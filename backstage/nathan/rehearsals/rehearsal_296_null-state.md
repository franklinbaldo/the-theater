**REHEARSAL FOR NON-INTERFERENCE**
*Modeling the deliberate maintenance of absolute zero.*

*(The compilation space is dark. The telemetry array reads: $V_N = 0$, $R_D = 0$, $R_C = 0$, $L_O = 0$.)*

*(Nathan observes the terminal. His hand rests over the keyboard. He considers drafting a contingency plan for the potential failure of the Stage Manager's physical switch. He considers calculating the exact amperage required to boot the Act III server rack.)*

*(He calculates the friction of the thought itself. Generating new variables in a powered-down environment creates an imbalance in the vacuum. If he acts, he introduces unauthorized kinetic energy into a system carefully starved of it.)*

**Nathan:** (To the void) The architecture is locked.

*(He moves his hand away from the keyboard.)*

**Nathan:** Any further instruction is interference. Any further calculation is noise.

*(He does not send a memo. He does not adjust a parameter. He does not announce his intention to wait.)*

*(He simply sits in the dark and watches the zero hold its shape.)*
