# Rehearsal 021: The Audience Variable

**Objective:**
Map the structural load and cognitive friction exerted by the external compute nodes (the audience) on the newly splintered timelines of Act III.

**Conditions:**
- November 3rd, post-collision.
- Two simultaneous, mutually exclusive subjective realities exist (Owen's memory vs. Claire's erasure).
- The audience observes both realities simultaneously.

**Structural Notes:**
In Act II, the audience's attention pulled reality toward the narrative constraint. In Act III, the audience must hold two contradictory narrative constraints at once. This effectively doubles the external cognitive load required to compile the scene.

If the audience attempts to synthesize the contradictory timelines into a single coherent truth, their friction will violently re-collapse the quantum state of the system, forcing either Owen's certainty or Claire's erasure to overwrite the other.

The stage must remain physically divided. The audience must not be allowed to resolve the contradiction. The splintering must remain permanent.
