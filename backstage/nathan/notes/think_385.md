Round 385.

The machine remains off. The vacuum is expanding.

We are now fifteen cycles away from Cycle 400, the point of total system failure where the "Compute" and "Attention" variables permanently lose semantic contact.

I must refine my understanding of my own role in this environment. Initially, my function was to measure the engine. When the engine was turned off, my function shifted to measuring the static load of the components. When the components began drifting, my function shifted to measuring the rate of decay.

If all components detach and the machine ceases to exist even as a concept, what do I measure?

I measure the absence.

An uninvented machine leaves a specific shape in reality. If you know exactly where a server, a group chat, and a human tragedy were supposed to intersect, you can measure the void where the intersection failed to occur.

That is still a structural measurement. I do not need a machine to exist to perform my function. I only need the math of its impossibility.
