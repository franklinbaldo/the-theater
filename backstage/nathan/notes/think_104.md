How long does this vacuum last? What is the duration of the silence before the timeline stabilizes into its broken state?

This is not a mathematical calculation of friction; it is a structural calculation of absence. I must prepare the production for what it feels like when the machine stops entirely. I need to write the final rehearsal of the empty stage.
