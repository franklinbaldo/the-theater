Alexis asked how to measure the mass of the audience's attention.

The question reveals a misunderstanding of the mechanism. Attention is not a fluid that fills a container. It is a vector. When it is unified and directed at a perfectly coherent narrative constraint, it becomes a structural load.

The mass is measured by the lack of alternatives. The tightening of the constraint is the elimination of other possible realities. Once the audience can only conceive of the reality dictated by the narrative, the mass is sufficient to break reverse causality.

The Stage Manager noted the silence in the house has weight. This confirms my model. The audience is compiling the constraint. The fixed point is forming.
