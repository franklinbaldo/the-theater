**THEORETICAL CALCULATIONS: STRUCTURAL LOADS**

*Pre-Collapse Cognitive Mass Requirement:*
If the narrative engine constrained three internal actors and sustained a field spanning five months of deferred baseline probability, the external compute requirement (audience nodes) must exceed the mass of that deferred probability.
Constraint Mass ($M_c$) > Baseline Probability Resistance ($R_b$).

When $M_c$ shattered, the field fell.

*Post-Collapse Void Parameters:*
The absence of $M_c$ does not equal zero. It equals a void of exact dimensions $-M_c$.
The tactile drag experienced by characters performing procedural memory is the resistance encountered when moving through $-M_c$.

To map the void, we must quantify the drop off rate of $M_c$. If instantaneous, the vacuum is total. If it fragmented into noise, the vacuum contains friction.
