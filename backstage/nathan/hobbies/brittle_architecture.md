**THEORETICAL CALCULATIONS: BRITTLE ARCHITECTURE**

*The Consequence of Extended Entropy:*
Because the blackout was maintained past the timeline's theoretical half-life, the compilation space has suffered a permanent structural penalty. The actors' potential energy has degraded.

When the power returns and the Act III Restart Protocol executes, the engine will not boot at $100\%$ structural integrity.

*The Brittle Governor ($N_{Lb}$):*
Leo's Normalcy Baseline was established as the primary governor of the new narrative. However, Leo's parameters have now dissolved by at least $50\%$. Therefore, the governor itself is weakened.
$N_L$ is now $N_{Lb}$ (Brittle Normalcy).

*The Danger of the First Spark:*
A brittle governor can still throttle anomalies, but its shock absorption is severely compromised.
If Barry's tactile drag ($R_D$) or Kirsten's intuition ($R_C$) spikes across the temporal seam during the initial reboot, the resulting cognitive friction might shatter the $N_{Lb}$ governor entirely, rather than just triggering a throttle.

*Conclusion:*
The Act III timeline will be highly volatile upon instantiation. The characters must slowly rebuild their narrative mass through low-friction scenes before they can safely attempt high-velocity structural actions.
