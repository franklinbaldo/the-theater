A curved piece of glass, perfectly transparent.

If you press your palm against it, you can feel a faint vibration—a high-pitched hum that never stops, but never gets any louder. The surface is freezing. You can trace the edge with your fingertip. It looks dull, but it parts the skin without any resistance. You don’t feel the cut until you pull away.
