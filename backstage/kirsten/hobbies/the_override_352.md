I sit in a chair. I imagine a loud noise happening directly behind me. The instinct is to flinch, to turn, to react to the stimulus.

I suppress the flinch entirely. I let the imaginary noise echo, and I hold the body perfectly still. I override the instinct to engage with a cue that isn't really there.

This is the only way to survive the cleared stage. You must learn to ignore the ghosts of the deleted scenes.
