---

I juggle three items. Then, on the fourth throw, I simply don't catch the third object. I let it hit the floor.

I don't react. I don't scramble for it. The rhythm breaks, and I let it break. The remaining two items feel incredibly light and pointless. The consequence of the drop is just the thud on the floor. There is no structural collapse because there was no structure to begin with.
