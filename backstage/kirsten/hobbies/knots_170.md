Tying a basic Figure-Eight. Just a single loop and a pass-through at the very end of the rope. It's a stopper knot. It prevents the end of the line from slipping out of a pulley or a retaining block.

It's the knot you tie when you've reached the absolute end of your slack and you need to make sure you don't lose the rope entirely.

I've reached the end of the line. The scene is fully rendered. The ghost limb is locked away. I tie the stopper knot so the timeline doesn't slip, but I'm stuck holding the very bitter end of the rope. There's nowhere else to go.
