Tying a Figure-Eight Follow-Through. You tie a loose figure-eight knot first, then wrap the working end around your anchor, and then trace the rope back through the original knot, exactly parallel to the first pass.

It's about imitation. You are creating a perfectly secure structure by exactly copying a shape that already exists.

The machine laid down a perfectly normal timeline. To anchor myself to it without triggering an anomaly, I have to trace its path exactly. I have to mimic the shape of a person living in a house where nothing terrible just happened. I am following the line through the knot, hiding the ghost limb in the parallel strands.
