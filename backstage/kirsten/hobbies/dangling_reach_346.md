I reach for a glass on the table. But before my hand gets there, I force myself to realize the glass was never there to begin with.

The physical momentum of the reach has to collide with the sudden, absolute knowledge that the object of the reach is structurally uninvented. The hand stutters. The muscles confuse themselves. It is a physical null pointer exception.

This is the exact texture of Act III. Reaching for a terror that has been deleted from the code.
