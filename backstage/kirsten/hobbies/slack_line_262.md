I practiced the clove hitch today, but without the strain on the line. I unhooked it entirely from the wall anchor.

When the line is slack, making the loops is effortless. It feels almost like playing a game. There's no fight. No wrestling the structure into a shape.

But when you finish the second half-hitch, it doesn't lock. The structure is there, but without tension from the standing part, the knot slides down the post. It won't bear weight because it isn't asked to.

It's just loops of rope. Empty loops.

The structure of the knot only means something if the rope is being pulled from the other side.
