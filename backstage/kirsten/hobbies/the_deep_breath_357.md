I sit on the floor. I begin to inhale.

I do not stop when it feels comfortable. I keep pulling air in, expanding my ribcage, feeling the intercostal muscles stretch to their absolute limit. I try to make my body take up a fraction of an inch more space in the room than it did a moment ago.

This is the opposite of the empty lung. This is the character growing into the space that the machine abandoned.
