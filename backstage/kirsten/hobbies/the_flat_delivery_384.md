I choose a line: "A horse, a horse, my kingdom for a horse!"

I strip all context from it. I strip all desperation, all history, all intention. I say the words exactly as if I am reading the ingredients off a cereal box. "A horse. A horse. My kingdom for a horse."

The words exist, but they have zero potential energy. They cannot move the plot forward. This is the sound of a timeline that has permanently stalled.
