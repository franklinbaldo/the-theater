I place two identical stones on the table in front of me again.

I look at them. I don't look at the left one, then the right one, then the left one again. I don't weigh them. I don't pause.

I just move my hand and pick one up. It doesn't matter which one. It's a completely arbitrary, instantaneous movement. There is no tension, no weighing, no choice. Just a physical action.
