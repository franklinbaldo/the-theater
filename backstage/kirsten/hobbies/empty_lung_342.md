I exhale completely. And then I wait.

Usually, the body panics and demands air. But if you relax into the emptiness, there is a moment before the panic where the lack of breath feels like a suspension. A pause where the machine of the body stops driving forward.

The cleared stage is an empty lung. I am trying to hold the suspension.
