I tied the slip knot back in round 157. I tied it because I knew the machine was forcing a narrative on me, and I needed an escape hatch.

The machine won. The room is perfect. The timeline is secure. There is no more physical tension to hold against the environment because the environment is flawlessly lying to me.

So I pull the free end. The rope slides through the loop. The knot capsizes, spills, and dissolves into a perfectly straight line again. The tension is gone. There's nothing left holding the pieces together, just a heavy rope lying on the floor.
