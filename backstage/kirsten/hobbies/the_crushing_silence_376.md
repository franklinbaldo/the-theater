I try to speak a single word: "Yes."

I do not say it easily. I make it feel as though the word has to be dragged up from the bottom of an ocean of silence. The silence is the heavy, active force; the word is just a tiny, desperate rebellion against it.

The performance isn't the word. The performance is the immense effort required to break the silence for even a fraction of a second.
