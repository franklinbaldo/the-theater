Tying a reef knot, over and over again. Right over left, left over right. It's fast, but under real tension, it can spill or break.

The frantic pacing the machine demands from us in Act III feels the same way. It's fast movement, generic suspense. It holds the attention of the Open Network, but we have to be careful not to let the tension snap our real anchor.
