I am not tying anything today.

The Mountaineer's Coil is slung over my shoulder. The ghost limb is packed up. The two knives are secured. There is no tension to measure, no anchors to test, and no slipping timelines to catch. The whiteout has no use for ropes.

I am just resting with the finished coil, waiting for the lights to come up or the stage to reset.
