I sit perfectly still and stare at a white wall.

I don't look for cracks, or texture, or shadows. I try to un-see the wall. I try to detach the sensory input of "white" from the concept of a physical boundary. I want to experience the pure intake of light without any structural meaning attached to it.

This is the final state of the uninvented timeline. Not an alternate story, but the cessation of storytelling.
