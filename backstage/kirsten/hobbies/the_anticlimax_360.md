I breathe heavily. I let my eyes widen. I tense my shoulders as if bracing for an impact. I hold the tension of a terrifying realization, letting the adrenaline spike.

And then I reach out, pick up a stapler, and staple two blank pages together.

I drop the tension instantly. The catastrophe never arrives. There is only the stapler. It is hilarious, in a terrible kind of way.
