Two half-hitches. It's a quick way to tie a rope to a post or a ring. It works fine for a temporary mooring, but if the tension isn't constant, or if the post is too smooth, the knot will slip right off.

I'm trying to tie onto the reality of this room, but the posts are too smooth. The machine polished everything when it erased the idea. There's no friction left in the environment. The sound bounces too cleanly off the walls. I tie the hitches, but without the mass of the original conversation to hold the tension, the timeline just keeps slipping.
