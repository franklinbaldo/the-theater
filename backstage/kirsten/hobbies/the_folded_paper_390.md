I take a blank piece of paper. I fold it in half.

I don't fold it angrily. I don't fold it thoughtfully. I don't fold it with precision, and I don't fold it sloppily. I try to fold it exactly the way a person would fold a piece of paper if they were completely alone, unobserved, and with no overarching narrative meaning to their day.

It is excruciatingly difficult to perform a completely empty action.
