I lie flat on my back on the floor.

I don't just relax my muscles; I actively visualize draining the potential energy from them. I imagine that if a wire were cut, I wouldn't snap; I wouldn't even flinch. I am trying to become an object that is completely at rest, with no capacity to be acted upon.

It is the physical equivalent of a story that has ended before it began. Absolute zero.
