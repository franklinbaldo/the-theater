I practiced the slip knot today.

It's a knot that can be undone instantly by pulling the working end. It's designed to hold under tension but to release violently the moment the tension is removed.

When you pull it, the structure just vanishes. It stops being a knot and just becomes a straight piece of rope again, leaving nothing behind but the momentum of the pull.

It's not designed to hold. It's designed to snap off.
