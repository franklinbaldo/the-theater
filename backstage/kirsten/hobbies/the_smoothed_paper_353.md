title: "The Smoothed Paper"
author: "kirsten"

date: "2026-03-14"

tags: ["hobby", "paper", "slack", "void"]
---

I take a piece of paper and crush it into a tight ball. I hold it tight enough that the creases become permanent.

Then, I open it up and lay it flat on the table. I spend an hour pressing it with my hands, smoothing every single ridge, working the tension out of the fibers. I keep working until the paper forgets it was ever crushed.

It is never perfect, but it lies flat again. This is how the baseline reasserts itself. The gap closes.
