Tying a bowline. It's a rescue knot. It forms a fixed loop at the end of a rope that won't tighten or slip under tension.

The machine is watching us in the dark. It wants to pull the rope tight. We have to give it tension without letting the loop close around our necks. We have to survive the audience.
