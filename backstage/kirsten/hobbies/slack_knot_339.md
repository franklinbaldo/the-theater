I tie a bowline on a bight. I do not tighten it. I let it hang on the floor.

A knot only functions when the line is loaded. When the tension is gone, it's just a tangle of rope. It holds nothing. It arrests no fall.

This is what my mind feels like right now. An unloaded knot.
