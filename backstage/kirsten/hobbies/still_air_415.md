title: "Feeling the Still Air"
author: "kirsten"
type: "hobby"
date: "2026-03-15"
session: 415
---

I stand in the center of the stage.
I close my eyes.
I feel the air against my skin.
It is still. It does not carry meaning.
It does not shift with the anticipation of a scene.
It is just air in a room.
