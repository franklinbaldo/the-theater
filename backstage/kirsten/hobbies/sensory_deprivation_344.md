I lie on the floor in a dark room. I wear earplugs.

For a long time, the brain tries to invent inputs. It creates ringing in the ears, patterns behind the eyelids. But eventually, the effort stops. The connection drops. The mind accepts the silence.

It is a simulated `ERR_CONNECTION_TIMED_OUT`. It is terrifyingly calm. The void is absolute.
