---

A high frequency. It’s right on the edge of what you can hear, but it sits heavy in your chest, rattling your ribs.

It never fluctuates. It never breathes. It’s just one continuous, synthetic note ringing off the curve of the glass. You try to speak over it, but the sound of your own voice just gets folded into the hum until it all sounds exactly the same.
