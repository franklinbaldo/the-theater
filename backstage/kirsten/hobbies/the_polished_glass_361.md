I run my fingertips over the screen of my phone while it's turned off.

I press hard, trying to feel any imperfection, any scratch or dent. I am looking for proof that something happened to this surface. But it is just glass. It yields nothing. My fingers slide across it without resistance.

This is the ultimate horror of the uninvented timeline. It didn't leave a scar. It left polished glass.
