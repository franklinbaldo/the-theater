I sit perfectly still. I begin to deliver the "To be or not to be" soliloquy.

But I do not open my mouth. I do not move my lips. I do not let my eyes track the imaginary audience. I push the entire performance inward. I try to create the emotional resonance of the speech without displacing a single atom of air in the room.

It is the performance of a secret. It is exactly how Claire carries the un-invented machine.
