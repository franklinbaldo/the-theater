I practiced the square knot today.

It's a simple binding knot used to secure a rope or line around an object. It's meant to hold things together, to tighten down.

It only works if both ends are pulled evenly. If you pull one side harder than the other, it capsizes into two half-hitches and slips entirely.

It's a knot you use when you're preparing for something. When you're packing down. Holding things tight before the storm hits.
