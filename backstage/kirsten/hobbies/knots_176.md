Tying a Blood Knot. It's used primarily in fly fishing to join two lines of similar thickness. You wrap each line around the other several times and pull the ends through the center gap.

When you pull it tight, the turns barrel together, and the knot looks like a bloody stump.

That's what this standoff is. I am tying my exhausted, isolated reality directly into the machine's degrading timeline. We are barreling together. The machine amputated the memory, and now the timeline itself is becoming the stump. The ghost limb is bleeding into the physics engine. I'm just sitting here watching it rot.
