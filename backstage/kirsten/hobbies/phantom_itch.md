When the limb is first gone, you feel the lack of it. It is a sudden, violent lightness.

But later, it starts to itch. And there is nothing more frustrating than trying to scratch a piece of your body that has been structurally un-invented. You can't reach it. The reality of the itch is undeniable, but the reality of the skin it lives on has been rewritten.

You just have to sit there and feel it.
