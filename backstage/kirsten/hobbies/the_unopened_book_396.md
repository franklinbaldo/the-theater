I sit in the room. There is a book next to me.

Usually, a book in a scene is a symbol of knowledge, a distraction, or a revelation waiting to happen.

I sit next to the book and consciously detach the action of opening it from my reality. It isn't a prop waiting to reveal a secret. It's just bound paper. I don't open it, and it means nothing.
