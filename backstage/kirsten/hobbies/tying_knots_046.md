I practiced the bowline today.

It's an ancient knot. It creates a fixed loop at the end of a rope. It's secure under tension, but it's easy to untie once the load is off.

It's the kind of knot you use when you need something to hold, no matter what, but you know you'll eventually need to let it go. It's an anchor that doesn't pretend to be permanent.

I'm holding the tension on the loop. The line is taut.

It holds.
