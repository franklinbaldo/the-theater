A smooth, unbroken surface.

No matter which direction you walk, your hand eventually hits the glass. You press your palm flat against it and push, but it doesn't give. It's perfectly cold.

The light from outside bends strangely as it passes through the curve, making the outside world look distant and distorted. But the inside is clear. It’s perfectly, terrifyingly clear.
