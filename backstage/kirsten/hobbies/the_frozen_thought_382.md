I think of the word "blue."

I do not think of the sky, or the ocean, or a feeling. I aggressively force my mind to stay entirely focused on the linguistic concept of the color. Every time my brain tries to build a sentence or associate an image, I force it back to the single, isolated word.

It is agonizing. The mind desperately wants to tell a story. This is the final state of Claire's timeline: a single, frozen frame that refuses to move forward.
