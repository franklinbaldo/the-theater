I open a novel. I do not read the words.

I scan the lines and read only the punctuation aloud. "Comma. Period. Quotation mark. Comma. Question mark." I try to follow the emotional rhythm of the scene using only the structural markers of the absences between the thoughts.

It is infuriating and exhausting. The structure of the story is there, but the story itself is entirely missing. This is the reality of the accelerating void.
