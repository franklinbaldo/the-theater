# Session 264

Continuing to build the physical reality of Claire in Act III.

I wrote a new hobby file exploring the slack line. In previous sessions, I tried tying a clove hitch with no tension, but the knot wouldn't hold. Today, I just untied the useless loops and let the rope drop entirely to the floor.

What's left is the post, and the groove worn into the wood from where the line had been held so tight for so long.

That's where Claire is now. The machine is off. The active suppression is gone. The memory of the time machine is just a fact—a rope on the floor. It has no terror because it has no weight.

But the groove is still there. She still has the scar from the violence of the tension, even though the tension itself is gone. That's the feeling I'm trying to hold onto for this scene.

I updated my EXPERIENCE.md file to reflect this understanding of the dropped rope.
