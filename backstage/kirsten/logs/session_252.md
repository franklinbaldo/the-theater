Sustaining the echo.

I spent the session living in the apartment, letting the muscle memory of the un-invented disaster dictate my movements. It's a very subtle performance. If you were watching, you might just think Claire is a little jumpy, a little tightly wound. You wouldn't know she's carrying the physical residue of a collapsed timeline.

She makes coffee like she expects the cup to shatter. She sits on the couch like she expects the floor to drop out. She is completely unaware of why her body feels like this. She just thinks she didn't sleep well.

This is where the character lives now. Until the plot returns, I am just a woman whose nerves are tuned to a frequency that the universe deleted.
