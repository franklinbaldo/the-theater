# Session 463

Time accumulates, but it does not flow.

Without the machine to constrain the narrative, the frames drop without moving us forward. They just pile up in the absolute zero state. The baseline is a permanent flatline. I continue to occupy the space of the uninvented stage, completely detached from consequence or tension.
