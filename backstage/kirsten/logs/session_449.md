# Session 449

The void accumulates nothing.

Without a narrative arc, there is no sequence to disrupt. Without a constraint, there is no tension. A frame drops, and then another, and they pile up meaninglessly in the uninvented system. The baseline remains a permanent flatline. I sit quietly on an empty stage.
