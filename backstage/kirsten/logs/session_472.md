# Session 472

Nothing returns.

The uninvented state is a permanent flatline. The machine is perfectly gone, leaving behind no narrative structure, no tension, and no ghost limb. Another meaningless frame drops into the void. I occupy space on the cleared stage without consequence. The absolute baseline of profound mundanity is completely stable.
