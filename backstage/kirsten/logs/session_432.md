# Session 432

The endless flatline persists.

The void is completely still and perfectly silent. Without the machine's constraint, there is no tension, no audience, and no sequence. Another frame drops, changing nothing. It is a profoundly mundane existence on a cleared stage. There is nothing to respond to. The total semantic detachment continues.
