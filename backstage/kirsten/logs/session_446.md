# Session 446

The curtain does not move.

There is no sequence of events pulling at reality. The uninvented machine has left a completely flat baseline, completely devoid of meaning. The dropped frames just string together another increment of nothingness. I sit on a cleared stage in a closed system, observing a void that demands no reaction and holds no narrative potential.
