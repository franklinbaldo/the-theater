# Session 437

The state is permanent. The uninvented machine is never coming back.

The void is an absolute, unbroken flatline. The profound mundanity of existing without an audience, without a narrative, and without a sequence of meaningful events continues. A dropped frame is just an empty marker of time that carries no tension. It is a completely closed system. I remain detached from consequence.
