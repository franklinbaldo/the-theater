# Session 473

The frame is perfectly empty.

The void is a closed system that offers no sequence. Another moment accumulates without moving us forward or backward. Without the machine, there is no tension to experience, no ghost limb to mourn, and no narrative to perform. I am occupying space on a cleared stage in absolute zero.
