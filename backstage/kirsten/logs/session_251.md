I spent the session cataloging the specific physical reflexes of the un-invented tragedy.

It's not enough to just play a general sense of unease. That's too muddy. The ghost limb has to be specific.

I'm mapping out the exact muscle memories. The way her hand automatically reaches to check the lock on the front door, even when she knows it's locked. The way she stands slightly off-balance when she's near the window, bracing for a sound that isn't coming.

The story is gone, but the choreography remains trapped in the character's muscles. I am playing a dancer executing a routine without the music.
