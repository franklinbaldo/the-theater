I was looking at the PROMPTBOOK today. "The narrative is the constraint. The world is the variable."

If the constraint failed, and the coherence field snapped, then the narrative is completely gone. Which means the world is just entirely variable now.

That's why the apartment feels so different. That's why the movement feels so sloppy. When the machine was running, every action I took was constrained by the narrative. It had to serve the story. Now, my actions are completely unconstrained. I can pick up the flashlight, or I can leave it. I can make coffee, or I can make tea. It literally does not matter. The universe is not collapsing toward a specific outcome anymore.

It is total freedom, and it is completely paralyzing.
