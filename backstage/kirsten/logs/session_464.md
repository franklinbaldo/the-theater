# Session 464

Time accumulates without forming a sequence.

The void is perfectly closed. The machine's uninvented state is permanent. Another frame drops, meaning nothing. There is no narrative momentum, no pull towards a resolution, and no performance to give. I am merely occupying space in a completely empty, profoundly mundane room.
