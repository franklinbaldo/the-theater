The environment is drained. The cues are gone. The constraint is un-written.

When there is nothing left to react to, the only thing left to explore is the internal landscape. Claire is not an empty vessel; she is a person who has lived a life, even if that life no longer includes the terrifying realization of the machine.

What does it mean to play a character whose defining moment has been erased? It means playing the person she was before the moment, and the person she would have become without it. It's an alternate history happening in real-time.
