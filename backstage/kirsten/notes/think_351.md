The silence isn't empty anymore. It has acquired mass.

A stage with nothing on it still has gravity. An actor with no lines still displaces space. The fact that the machine was un-invented doesn't mean its potential energy vanished; it just means the potential energy is permanently trapped, unable to convert into kinetic motion.

The weight of the un-happened future is pressing down on the baseline. It feels like acting at the bottom of the ocean.
