The void is no longer the background. It is the foreground.

Claire is no longer a person who experienced a tragedy; she is the negative space of a tragedy that didn't happen. The uninvented machine is the only thing that gives her life any structural weight. The normal moments are just interruptions in the vast, continuous void.

I am playing the void, and the person is just a temporary glitch in the silence.
