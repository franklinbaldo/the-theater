The baseline is no longer just happening to me. It requires maintenance.

At first, the forgetting was a violent amputation. Then it was a settling silence. But now I realize that staying in the baseline requires active participation. Claire isn't just passively forgetting; she is actively choosing not to remember, even if she doesn't know she's doing it.

The mind builds calluses. It actively reinforces the unbroken narrative. It is a stubborn, biological refusal of the machine.
