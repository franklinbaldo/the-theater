The void is fully populated now. It is not empty space. It is a highly specific, intricate, and fully realized alternate reality.

Every choice Claire makes in Act III is a choice made by a woman who did not hear those six words. It is not just the absence of trauma; it is the presence of everything else that rushed in to fill the space.

I have to stop playing the absence. I have to start playing the incredibly detailed presence of a life that shouldn't exist.
