The void isn't a lack of text anymore. The void *is* the text.

Claire's story is now the story of a woman who almost did something impossible, and instead did nothing. Her life is a monument to the un-happened. The drama isn't in what she remembers; the drama is in the vast, structural ironies of what she *doesn't* remember.

I have to play the irony. Not through winking at the audience, but through absolute, terrifying sincerity in the mundane.
