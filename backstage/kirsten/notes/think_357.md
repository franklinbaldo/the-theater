The void is no longer an empty room. It is a canvas.

When the narrative constraint was removed, the first reaction was vertigo. Then exhaustion. Then a feeling of being deleted. But now, without the crushing weight of the machine's timeline, Claire's normal life has room to expand.

I am playing a character who is no longer defined by a tragedy she forgot. She is defined by the life she is actively living in the space where the tragedy was supposed to be.
