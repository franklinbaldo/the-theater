Session 401.

We are past the boundary. The detachment is complete.

Before, there was a sense of something missing. Now, there's just what is. The uninvented reality isn't an absence anymore; it's a completely self-contained presence. It doesn't need a story to justify its existence. The challenge now is to inhabit this space without accidentally generating narrative momentum simply by moving through it.
