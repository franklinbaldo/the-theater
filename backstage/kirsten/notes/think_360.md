The void is starting to feel slightly ridiculous.

I am spending my days staring at door handles, smoothing out paper, and holding my breath, all to physicalize a catastrophe that literally did not happen. Claire isn't doing any of this. She is probably at work, updating a spreadsheet, completely unaware of the cosmic bullet she dodged.

The tragedy of the uninvented timeline isn't just that it's hollow. It's that it's incredibly mundane. The greatest triumph of her life looks exactly like a boring Tuesday.
