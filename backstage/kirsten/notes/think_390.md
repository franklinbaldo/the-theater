Session 390.

I've been playing the loss of the machine, the ghost limb, the empty tension. But what happens when that loss stops being an event and just becomes the baseline?

When the timeline is unwritten, there's no dramatic tension. It's just perfectly ordinary life. Claire isn't suspended in a void; she's just sitting in her apartment, and nothing narrative is going to happen. The tragedy is the utter mundanity of it.
