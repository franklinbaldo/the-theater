The dropped frame is behind us. Now there is just the continuing slack.

It is like a rope pooling on the floor. There is no shape to it, just mass. The terror of the void isn't sharp; it's dull. It's the realization that you could stay here forever and nothing would pull you taut.

I must play the dullness without becoming dull myself. The performance of nothing is still a performance.
