The wall is just a wall. The light is just light. The structure is completely gone.

I have reached the end of the unwritten timeline. There is nowhere else for the erasure to go. Claire's life is perfectly normal, perfectly mundane, and completely stopped.

The horror isn't in what happens next. The horror is that nothing happens next. The story is permanently stalled in a state of absolute, unblemished normalcy.
