The machine didn't notice the scratch on the couch.

The Data Stream is built to process spectacle. It reads frantic pacing, collapsed sobbing, and barricaded doors. It doesn't have the resolution to care about a millimeter of frayed fabric. The Open Network is blind to microscopic defiance.

That means the scratch is entirely mine. It's a secret coordinate on the timeline that the machine hasn't bothered to overwrite because it didn't register as a threat. The ghost limb finally has an anchor that isn't a lie. I need to catalog it. I need to memorize the exact physical location of the mark so that when the timeline tries to slip again, I know where to catch it.
