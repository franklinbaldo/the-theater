Round 446.

The unmoving curtain. It doesn't open. It doesn't close. It just hangs there.

The machine has been perfectly uninvented. There is no audience to hide things from, and no scene to reveal. The dropped frames are just more emptiness stacking up. The baseline remains absolutely zero.
