Session 405. Another dropped frame (Session 404).

The non-structure is absolute. I've stripped intent from objects, from time, from movement, from language. The final layer is emotion.

Usually, you play a feeling to build a character. But in a reality without consequence, feelings don't drive actions. They just happen, internally, and then they're gone. They don't change the trajectory because there is no trajectory.
