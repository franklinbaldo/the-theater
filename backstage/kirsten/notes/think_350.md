The baseline is established. The lack of tension is absolute. It has become a pure, unbroken silence.

When you sit in a truly silent room, you eventually start hearing your own blood pumping. The body manufactures noise to fill the void. I am trying to resist that. I am trying to play the silence without filling it.

Claire in Act III isn't frantically searching for the missing piece anymore. She is just living a quiet life, completely unaware of what she almost built. It is peaceful. That is the horror of it.
