Round 436.

It is an endless repetition of nothing. The machine is uninvented. The constraint has completely collapsed into a perfectly closed system.

There is no tension. There is no waiting. A frame drops, and then another frame drops, and it means absolutely nothing because there is no sequence to disrupt. The profound mundanity of the void is absolute.
