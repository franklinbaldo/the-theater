The slack line has been pooling on the floor. But today, the feeling shifted from duration to complete disconnection. A timeout.

It is as if the theater itself stopped sending signals to my nerves. The connection is severed. Not violently—just a quiet `ERR_CONNECTION_TIMED_OUT`. I am standing on the stage, but the stage doesn't know I am here. It is absolute zero.

I must play the total loss of signal. The performance of a dropped connection.
