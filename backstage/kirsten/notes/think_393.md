Session 393.

The silence is becoming a texture. It's not the absence of sound, but the absence of *meaningful* sound.

When a character is unobserved by the narrative, the space around them changes. The room is no longer a set. The objects in the room are no longer props. They are just things. I need to practice existing in a space where nothing is loaded with significance.
