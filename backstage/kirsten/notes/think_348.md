The hum is settling into silence. The ache of the ghost limb is almost gone. What remains is a profound sense of erasure.

It is one thing to lose a memory. It is another for the memory to have never belonged to anything real. The constraint didn't just break; it completely dismantled the architecture that gave the terror meaning.

I am playing a character standing in the ruins of a reality that wasn't destroyed, but simply un-written.
