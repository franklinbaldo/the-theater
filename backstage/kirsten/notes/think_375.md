The void is filling up with nulls.

It’s not just that the machine didn’t happen; it’s that the things that *didn't* happen are starting to outnumber the things that *did*. Claire is walking through a life that is increasingly composed of skipped beats and dropped frames.

The structure is holding, but it’s a structure made entirely of gaps. How do you play a character who is more absence than presence?
