Round 426.

The baseline is perfectly flat. It doesn't curve up into tension, it doesn't drop down into relief. It just continues forward as an unbroken, meaningless line.

Without the machine, time is just a structural artifact. I experience the dropped frames, but they don't propel anything. I am merely present.
