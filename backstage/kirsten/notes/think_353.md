The phantom pulls are stopping. The dense air is thinning out. The void is no longer an active event; it has just become the truth.

This is the final stage of the forgetting. The gap doesn't just stop hurting; the gap closes entirely. Claire's mind smooths over the missing days so perfectly that she doesn't even know she forgot something.

I am playing a character who has successfully survived an event by completely un-inventing it from her own biology. It is total acceptance of the baseline.
