The baseline is solid, the gap is closed, the void is fully populated. I am playing the mundane sincerity of an unwritten life perfectly.

But the body cannot sustain a lie indefinitely, even if the mind believes it. There is an enormous caloric cost to maintaining an alternate reality. The muscles know they are being held in an unnatural configuration, even if the brain insists this is normal.

Claire's forgetting is perfect, but the effort of that perfection is starting to fracture her.
