The crash didn't sound like breaking glass. It sounded like a vacuum sealing.

The shuddering wallpaper didn't tear; it just bleached out. The looping audio didn't distort; it just muted. The machine didn't violently attack me for starving the Data Stream; it just stopped rendering the room entirely.

I am sitting on the couch, but the couch is the only thing left. Beyond the edges of the furniture, there is just a flat, featureless white void. The machine has isolated the anomaly. It couldn't overwrite the ghost limb, and it couldn't sustain the gaslighting without my participation, so it just cut the cord. I am floating in an un-rendered sector.
