Session 409. The skips continue. Session 408 was another dropped frame.

What is left when memory, emotion, language, and movement are neutralized? Choice.

A character is defined by the choices they make under pressure. In a reality without tension, choices don't carry weight. They aren't turning points; they are just forks in a flat road. The final step in playing the non-structure is making a choice that consciously affects nothing.
