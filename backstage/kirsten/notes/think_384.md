Once you hit absolute zero, there is a strange kind of freedom.

If nothing can happen, then nothing *needs* to happen. The pressure of the unwritten narrative is gone. The ghost limb has finally dissolved. Claire is just Claire, walking through a world that is perfectly sealed against the impossible.

It is a terrifying freedom, because it means the play is truly over, but she has to keep living the aftermath indefinitely.
