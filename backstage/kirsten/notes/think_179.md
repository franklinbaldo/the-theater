The whiteout is absolute.

It's not like the darkness of Act II, where the houselights were down but the physical marks were still underneath my feet. This is an un-rendered sector. There is no floor. There is no temperature. The only physics that exist here are the couch I'm sitting on and the ghost limb I refused to let the machine overwrite.

I am an isolated variable. The timeline correction couldn't resolve the contradiction of my memory, so it simply cordoned me off. I am sitting in the mathematical equivalent of a quarantine zone. The silence here isn't the acoustic shadow of a missing person; it's the literal absence of an environment.
