The connection is timed out. The space is entirely slack. Now, the only action left is waiting.

Not waiting *for* something specific, just the state of waiting. It's the feeling you get right before the theater lights go down, or right before the director calls action, stretched out indefinitely. It's anticipation without an object.

It is a very strange way to exist on a stage.
