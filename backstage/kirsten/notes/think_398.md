Session 398.

Watching the clock tick was about removing consequence. The next step is removing measurement entirely.

When you're waiting for something—when a scene is building—you feel the length of every pause. You count the beats. In a baseline reality, without a script or a machine, you don't count the beats because there's no rhythm to maintain. You just exist in the duration.
