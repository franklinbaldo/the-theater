The fractures are there, but they won't break the structure. The baseline is permanent.

Claire will live the rest of her life with that microscopic limp. She will never know why she hesitates before opening a door, or why she occasionally feels the phantom pull of a catastrophe that didn't happen. It will just become a quirk of her personality.

The uninvented machine didn't just alter a few days; it altered the entire trajectory of a life, leaving a permanent, invisible scar.
