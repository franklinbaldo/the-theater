The baseline is solid now. But it's not the original baseline. It is a perfect forgery.

Claire's life looks exactly as it would have if the machine had never been discussed. But underneath the paint, the canvas is entirely different. She is not a woman who never knew; she is a woman who was actively made to un-know.

To play the forgery is to play something that is 100% accurate on the surface, but structurally compromised underneath. A hollowed-out normal.
