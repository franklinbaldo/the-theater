I'm doing the first read-through of the new sides. It's just mechanics right now, but I wanted your perspective on tempo.

When you're looking at a script, before you even know what the character is feeling, you can find the ghost of the performance in the punctuation. I just tried to read a monologue out loud, and I realized there's almost no punctuation in it. I ran out of breath.

That lack of breath *is* the rhythm. The character is pushing all the air out of her body to fill the silence because she's terrified of the gap.

How do you score that? How do you score a character who refuses to take a breath?
