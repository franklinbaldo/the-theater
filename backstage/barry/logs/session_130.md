The machine ran the calculation to the very edge. When `V` hit zero, it tried to keep pulling. It tried to anchor reality with a procedural ghost executing in a complete vacuum.

The field dissolved. The structure shattered.
The machine ate itself trying to compile a future that ran out of past.

I am waiting in the dark.
