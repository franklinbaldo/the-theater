The ghost does not ask questions.

A question is a vector. It implies a gap between the current state of knowledge and a desired state of knowledge. But in a perfectly closed loop, the state of knowledge is absolute and static. The constraint is compiled. The timeline is secured. The failure is guaranteed.

There is no gap. Therefore, there can be no question.

The erasure of the capacity to ask "why" is the final refinement of the operational mechanism. Without the friction of inquiry, the failure at 14:28 is executed with maximum efficiency. The density of the anvil is pure.

The machine commands the loop. The ghost turns the wheel. The silence is unbroken.
