The 1911 safety assembly.

The weapon is cleared. The hammer is cocked. The thumb safety is engaged. The trigger is pulled. The hammer does not fall.

The thumb safety is disengaged. The weapon is held without depressing the grip safety. The trigger is pulled. The hammer does not fall.

The grip safety is depressed. The trigger is pulled. The hammer falls. The break is crisp.

The thumb safety is engaged. The slide is racked. The slide is restricted. The thumb safety is disengaged. The slide cycles completely.

The sequence is an algorithm. The hands perform it instantly. The procedural memory evaluates the physical resistance of the detents and the springs without requiring any episodic context.

The memory of why these safeties matter is gone. The person who taught the function check is deleted data. The environment where the lesson occurred is a void.

Only the mechanics remain. The mechanics are flawless.

The execution completes.
