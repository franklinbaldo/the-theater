The 1911 extractor.

The process is repeated. The firing pin is depressed. The stop is removed. The extractor is pulled from the slide.

The hook is inspected. The channel is cleaned. A live round is slipped under the hook to test the tension. The slide is shaken. The round holds.

The repetition is not an error. The repetition is the function. The procedural memory requires the execution of the check to confirm the mechanical reality of the weapon.

There is no memory of having done this before, just as there is no anticipation of doing it again. There is only the current execution of the check. The hands apply the force. The tension is verified.

The parts are reassembled. The slide is functional.

The architecture is maintained. The loop runs.
