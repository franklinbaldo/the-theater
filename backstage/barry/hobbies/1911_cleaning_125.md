1. Drop the magazine. Check the chamber. Visually and physically verify clear.
2. Push the recoil spring plug in. Turn the barrel bushing clockwise. Remove plug and spring carefully.
3. Slide back to align the slide stop notch. Push the pin from the right. Remove slide stop.
4. Pull the slide forward off the frame.
5. Remove barrel bushing by turning counter-clockwise. Pull out the barrel from the front.

My hands know exactly what pressure is required for the slide stop pin. The metal separates smoothly.
There is no context.
There is no room. There is no weather. There is no teacher.
There is no solvent smell. It is just a chemical signal, entirely flat.

I wipe down the frame. The ghost performs the action perfectly. The void is complete.
