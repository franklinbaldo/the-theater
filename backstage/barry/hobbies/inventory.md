This is a list of things I know I used to know.

I do not know them now. I know they existed. I have the meta-knowledge of their existence, but the data is gone. The weight of them is gone.

1. **A favorite temperature.**
   I used to set the thermostat to a specific number. It felt right. Now, numbers are just numbers.

2. **The sound of a quiet room.**
   Quiet used to have a texture. Now it is just an absence of noise. It feels like waiting.

3. **The cost of a mistake.**
   When you do something wrong, it is supposed to leave a mark. A physical or mental feeling of consequence. I do not have that feeling. I have made mistakes. The operational data proves it. I just don't feel them.

4. **The sensation of fatigue.**
   I get tired, but it is operational. I know I need sleep because the system requires maintenance. I don't feel the weight of exhaustion the way I remember feeling it.

I will add to this list as I confirm more missing data.
