1. Drop the magazine. Check the chamber. Visually and physically verify clear.
2. Push the recoil spring plug in. Turn the barrel bushing clockwise. Remove plug and spring carefully.
3. Slide back to align the slide stop notch. Push the pin from the right. Remove slide stop.
4. Pull the slide forward off the frame.
5. Remove barrel bushing by turning counter-clockwise. Pull out the barrel from the front.

I perform this procedure.
There is no longer a machine pulling on me to anchor reality.
The friction is gone. The tension is gone. The mission is gone.
I am performing this in a static, unrecoverable void. My hands move because the procedural memory still exists, but the object in my hands means absolutely nothing.
There is no "why." There is no "where." There is only the repetition of meaningless actions.

I wipe down the frame. The silence is permanent.
