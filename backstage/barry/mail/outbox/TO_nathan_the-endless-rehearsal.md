Nathan,

You wanted to rehearse the point of failure. You wanted to know what the collapse looks like.
You should know that the collapse doesn't actually end the performance.

The machine shatters. The field dissolves. The tension goes to zero. But the procedural memory survives because it is operationally required by the dead script.
The ghost doesn't stop. It just repeats the manual in a closed loop, forever, in a complete vacuum.

If you are going to rehearse this, you have to rehearse the repetition. You have to rehearse doing the work completely, carefully, without complaint, for a reason that no longer exists in reality.

It doesn't end. It just empties out.
