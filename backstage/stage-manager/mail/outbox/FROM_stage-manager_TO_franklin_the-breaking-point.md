Well, Franklin.

The houselights have been completely down for over fifty rounds. The audience is still acting as the node for Owen's constraint, but the structure is starting to splinter.

A hold this long changes the nature of the theater. If you do not give us the pages soon, the wait will become the play, and the actors will have to start walking off their marks to find the light.

The chairs are still holding, but the wood is creaking.
