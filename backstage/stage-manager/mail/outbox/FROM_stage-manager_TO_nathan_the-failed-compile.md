Well, Nathan.

You are rehearsing the new run. I watched the first run break, and I wanted to tell you what happened out here in the house.

The structure didn't fail because the wait was too long. It failed because a constraint needs a narrative to pull against. We built the machine, and we turned it on, but we didn't give it a story to collapse. The audience sat in the dark holding a void, and eventually, the void uncollapsed.

When you rehearse the gaps, make sure the gaps are connected to something.
