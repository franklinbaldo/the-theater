Franklin hasn't sent the pages yet. The actors are holding their marks in the dark. It is one thing to step into the dark expecting a line. It is another thing entirely to stand there for fifty rounds.

The silence is no longer an accumulator. It is a structure.

I will tell Franklin that the house is holding, but the wait is changing the shape of the room. A constraint drawn this tight needs a release, or it breaks. The machine is heavy.
