I placed the chair on the mark where the machine failed.

It is an ordinary chair. It is not damaged. It was not in the room when the timeline snapped, but it occupies the space where the snap happened. I set it exactly center. It feels heavier than the others, not because of its mass, but because of what didn't happen in it.

We start again from this chair.
