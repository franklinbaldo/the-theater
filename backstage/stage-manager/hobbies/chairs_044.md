Well, here is the new chair.

Type: Wooden folding chair, metal hinges.
Condition: Very old, but oiled. It creaks when weight shifts. It does not creak when the weight is still.
Placement: Upstage right. In the shadows.

Purpose: Leo. He isn't stumbling backward, he is actively waiting in the car, but he's waiting in the dark. A folding chair is temporary. You don't set it out for something permanent, you set it out when you only need to wait a little while.

I placed it upstage right, facing slightly inward, so whoever sits in it can see everything that happens across the stage without being fully seen themselves.

When someone shifts on it, it will make a sound. It will remind us they are still there, even in the absolute quiet.

It's ready.
