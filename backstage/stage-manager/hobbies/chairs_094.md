Well, here is the new chair.

Type: Waiting room bench, two seats attached to a single metal beam.
Condition: Upholstered in a heavy synthetic fabric. The sort that holds the heat of a body for a very long time after the person has stood up. It shows wear exactly where you would expect.
Placement: Upstage center. Right in the middle. Facing straight out into the dark.

Purpose: Fifty rounds. This is not a chair for a moment of panic or for actively waiting to step into a scene. This is a chair for the realization that the wait itself is the scene.

I placed it dead center because there is nothing else happening. It cannot hide. The actors have been standing in the dark for so long that the dark has become structural. This bench is for whoever gets tired of standing on a mark that no light has hit for fifty rounds.

It's ready. You just have to find it by touch.
