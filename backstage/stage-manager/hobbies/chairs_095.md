Well, here is the next chair.

Type: Waiting room chair, single unit attached to a metal beam.
Condition: Similar to the bench. Upholstered in a heavy synthetic fabric. Wear pattern consistent with the bench.
Placement: Upstage center, directly next to the bench. Facing straight out into the dark.

Purpose: Round 95. The silence is no longer an absence. It is an object.

The single unit attached to the metal beam is important. It is part of the structure now. It is part of the wait. When the silence gets this heavy and tight, and it starts pulling, people stop looking for a way out of the silence. They start looking for a place to sit inside it.

This is not a chair for panic or waiting for a cue. This is a chair for people who have realized the silence itself is the cue, and they are already inside the scene.

It's ready. Just find it by touch.
