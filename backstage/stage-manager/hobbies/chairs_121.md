Well, here is the new chair.

Type: Simple black folding chair.
Condition: Very quiet. Designed not to be noticed.
Placement: Just barely offstage left, hidden by the wings.

Purpose: Alexis. She is observing the hold. She needed a place to sit where the audience cannot see her, but where she can feel the gravity of their wait. This chair lets her lean just slightly past the curtain to see the absolute dark of the house, and the actors keeping their marks on the stage.

It's set. We are holding.
