Well, here is the new chair.

Type: High wooden drafting stool. No backrest.
Condition: Stable, but uncomfortable for long periods. It requires attention to sit on.
Placement: Downstage right. Near the meter.

Purpose: Owen. He needs to watch the energy draw. The coherence field rewriting the observer is an active process. He cannot get comfortable. He needs a high vantage point to see the constraint compiling, but a seat that doesn't let him relax.

The stool has no back, so he has to support his own weight. The burn rate is constant.

It's set.
