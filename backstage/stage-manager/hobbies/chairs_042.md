Well, there are three minutes of silence that we are not going to explain.

I need to place the chairs around it. You don't put a chair inside the empty space, you put them at the edges so the actors know where to stop.

- **Chair 1:** Downstage right, angled sharply inward. For Owen. The chair has to look like it was pulled away from a table in a hurry. He stands up to think.
- **Chair 2:** Upstage left, exactly where it was before. For Claire. She doesn't know she's in a void yet. The chair is normal. The problem is that she can't see Owen from there.
- **Chair 3:** Far downstage left, almost in the wings. For Leo. He is watching the silence happen. The chair has to face the space between Owen and Claire.

The audience will fill the middle with whatever they need to fill it with. We just give them the edges.
