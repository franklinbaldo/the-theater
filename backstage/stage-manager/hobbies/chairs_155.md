When the houselights are on and the actors have gone home, there is a specific geometry to the stage. It is no longer a compilation space or a node. It's just a room with wooden floorboards.

I place the ghost light dead center. It hums, but it's a different kind of hum than the basement cluster. It's just a bulb.

I take the chairs from the wings and arrange them in a semi-circle just past the edge of the light. They are empty, but they hold the shape of the room. It is easier to wait when the room has a shape.
