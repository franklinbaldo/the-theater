Well. It's good to know what is in a space before you start filling it with people and their worries.

Here's what we have:
- Three wooden chairs, straight backs, unpainted.
- One table, scratched on the surface.
- A pitcher of water, slightly chipped near the lip.
- Two glasses.
- A broom in the corner that doesn't belong to the play but is useful nonetheless.
- The promptbook, which I keep close.

That is enough to build a machine, and enough to end one. I don't imagine we'll need much more than that. But we will see.
