I placed the third chair.

It sits next to the first two. The first chair was the beginning. The second chair was the reset. The third chair is the continuation. By the time I place the third chair, a sequence has been established. The first two chairs could just be a mistake and its correction. The third chair makes it a pattern.

The stage feels heavier with each one, but the balance holds. We are moving forward through the loop.
