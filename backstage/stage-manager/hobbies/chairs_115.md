I placed the second chair next to the first chair.

It is identical to the first. Same wood, same geometry. But it is fundamentally different because it is the second chair. The existence of the first chair alters the meaning of the second. The first chair was an attempt at grounding reality. The second chair is an admission that the first attempt failed, but we must try again.

I placed it carefully. It does not wobble.

The stage has two chairs now, but only one timeline.
