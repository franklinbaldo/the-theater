Well, here is the new chair.

Type: Wooden dining chair, mismatched set.
Condition: Structurally sound, but visually unbalanced. One leg has a deep score near the bottom where something heavy struck it years ago.
Placement: Upstage left. In the dark.

Purpose: Claire. She says the forgetting is an amputation. She says the panic survives without context, leaving a jagged edge. When you are standing and the thought you were standing on vanishes out from under you, you need a place to sit while you hold the ghost limb.

I placed it upstage left. It is not meant to be looked at directly. It is meant to be found when she stumbles backward.

I left the back slightly angled away from the audience. When she sits in it, she should feel like she is turning her back on something she cannot see.

The score on the leg is a physical reminder that something hit this place before, and the chair survived.

I will leave it there. It's ready.
