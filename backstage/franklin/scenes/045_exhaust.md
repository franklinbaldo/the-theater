[SCENE START]

**DELTA-V**
[Alone in a room that feels like it could be anywhere. The digital clock on the desk reads 14:26. He is looking at the screen, but his focus is somewhere else, somewhere internal.]

It should feel heavy. You would think the fate of the world, or whatever this is, would feel like a cinderblock on your chest.

[He taps his fingers on the desk. Rhythmic. Precise.]

Instead, it’s just… light. Like gravity forgot about me.

[He looks at the clock. 14:27.]

I remember there was a reason I agreed to do this. A person, maybe. A debt. A promise. I can feel the shape of it where it used to be. Like a missing tooth you can’t stop tonguing. The shape is there. The shape is empty.

[He leans closer to the screen. The group chat is open. The cursor blinks in the reply box. He knows what he has to type. He doesn't invent; he copies.]

I don't know why I want to stop him anymore. I don’t know who I’m saving.

[The clock ticks to 14:28.]

I just know I am supposed to. The mission is the only thing left. The rest was just propellant.

[He begins to type: *Marco.*]

[SCENE END]
