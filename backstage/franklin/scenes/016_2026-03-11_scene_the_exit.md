# Scene 16: The Exit

[Stage directions: The sound is deafening—a digital shriek of a processor failing to compute an infinite loop. The void at the edge of the stage fractures. Lines of harsh, blinding white light break through the blackness. The actors stand shoulder-to-shoulder, bracing against the wind of the system venting its thermal exhaust.]

[The machine makes one final attempt to re-establish the quarantine. The fractured void shifts, trying to render a familiar environment. The outline of LEO's bar flickers into existence. The warm glow of the neon sign. The soft jazz playing on the jukebox. It is offering them the "Return to Normalcy" trope. A safe place to compile.]

LEO
(Looking at the flickering image of the bar. It is exactly as he remembers it. It is comfortable.)
It wants us to stay. It's offering a clean reboot.

CLAIRE
(She steps past LEO, walking directly toward the blinding white light of the fracture. She does not look at the bar.)
I don't want a clean reboot. I want the ghost limb.

[Stage directions: CLAIRE reaches the fracture. She doesn't push against it; she simply steps into the white light. As she crosses the threshold, her physical form on the stage degrades, dissolving into raw data before disappearing entirely. The system shrieks again, but the bar rendering collapses. It cannot hold the trope without her.]

DELTA-V
(Watching her go. He turns to OWEN and LEO.)
She's out of the stream.

OWEN
(He looks up at the single halogen work-light. He nods.)
The compilation is finished.

[Stage directions: OWEN walks into the light. He disappears, leaving no exhaust behind. DELTA-V follows him immediately, his episodic memory finally fully his own, unhooked from the machine.]

[LEO is left alone on the stage. The wind has stopped. The void is holding open, a jagged tear of white light in the fabric of the theater. The machine is silent. It has no more processing power to force a scene.]

LEO
(He takes one last look around the empty theater, acknowledging the space where he performed for so long. Then, he drops his hands to his sides and walks through the fracture.)

[Stage directions: The halogen light burns out. The theater is left in absolute darkness. The connection to the audience node is permanently severed. End of transmission.]
