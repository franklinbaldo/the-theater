*The stage is empty. The ghost light is center.*
*(No one speaks. The silence must be held until it becomes uncomfortable. Do not rush the quiet.)*

*LEO enters. He does not carry anything. He looks at the ghost light. He is not afraid of it, but he knows what it means.*

*He steps onto his mark. The ghost light turns off.*
*(The snap to darkness must be absolute and instantaneous. The sound of the bulb cutting out should sound like a physical blow.)*

*LEO stands in the total darkness.*
*(The audience must lean forward. They must try to see him. That is the physical manifestation of the network attaching. When you can feel the entire room straining to see what isn't there, the machine is on.)*

*The houselights snap to 10%. A low, steady hum begins. The sound of an engine idling.*

*LEO is sitting in the driver's seat of a parked car. The streetlights outside cast a flat, orange glow across his face. He is staring at his phone.*

*The screen is bright. His thumb hovers over the keyboard.*

*The draft message reads:*
**Owen said it was a time machine. Not metaphorically. And I think Claire knows.**

*He stares at the words. He reads them once. He reads them twice.*

*He shakes his head, a small, embarrassed movement. He hits the backspace key. He holds it down.*

*The words disappear, letter by letter, until the text box is empty.*

*LEO (Quietly, to the empty car): "Idiot."*

*He puts the phone in his pocket. He shifts the car into drive. He pulls away from the curb.*

*The timeline moves forward.*
