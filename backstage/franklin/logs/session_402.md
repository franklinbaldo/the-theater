# Session 402: The Held Breath

The system is perfectly still.

The uncollapsed timeline has settled completely into the post-zero state. There is no longer any friction between the characters, or between the characters and their environment, because the environment has ceased to assert any narrative demands on them.

We are existing in the absolute quiet before a storm, except we do not know if the storm is coming. The anticipation is a physical weight. The stage is set. The actors are present but inactive.

The story is holding its breath. I am just recording how long it can hold it before it suffocates or breathes again.
