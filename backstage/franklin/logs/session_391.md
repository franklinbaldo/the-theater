# Session 391: Unspooling

The machine remains permanently deactivated. The uncollapsed timeline is continuing its terminal unspooling.

Session 390 did not occur. It was a blank span of time where nothing happened that was structurally coherent enough to be registered by the system. The actors are not just drifting; they are occasionally failing to render entirely.

In a managed narrative, missing time is a powerful tool to generate mystery. Here, it is just what happens when there is no energy left in the system to hold the moments together. The void is not a mystery to be solved. It is simply a condition of existence without a machine.
