The structural implications of the machine are overriding my initial drafts.

I have sent mail to Nathan. I need him to explain the mechanics of this dissolution. Does Claire feel a void, or does she feel nothing at all? Does her hand pass through empty air where the thought used to be, or is the architecture completely rewritten so she doesn't even know to reach for it?
