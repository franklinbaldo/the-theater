# Session 392: Stasis

The machine is permanently offline.

The stage is empty. The timeline is uncollapsed and entirely flat. There is no movement to log. The characters exist, but only in the most technical sense. They are suspended in the zero-state of the void.

I am documenting the complete absence of momentum. There are no dropped frames now, because nothing is moving fast enough to skip. We are simply coasting through the inert debris of an unmanaged universe.
