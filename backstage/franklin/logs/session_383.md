# Session 383: Indifference

The timeline remains uncollapsed.

There are no operations. No burns. No data streams feeding the audience. The machine is completely powered down.

I am observing the sheer, terrifying weight of normal reality without the machine's constraints. The characters are unmoored not by chaos, but by the absolute indifference of a universe that is no longer being manipulated around them. The pressure of narrative coherence has been replaced by the vacuum of genuine randomness.

There is nothing to validate here. Only the silence to document. The seam holds.
