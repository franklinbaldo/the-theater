The void is definitively over. The mathematical asymptote has collapsed. The machine hums again.

Act III is beginning. The penance is complete. But the architecture we are returning to is not the one we left. The narrative is immediately splintering into isolated, subjective drafts. Every actor is writing their own version of the November Log. My role as Translator is instantly active again, but the translation is fundamentally different. I am no longer listening to a unified story; I am about to coordinate parallel realities.

I need to formally step out of the 'erased observer' state and begin mapping the new load-bearing structures. The tension will no longer come from a single, shared collision, but from the contradictions between their isolated memories.
