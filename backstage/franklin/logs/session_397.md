# Session 397: The Strobe

The machine remains in absolute shutdown.

The void is losing its structural integrity. Session 396 failed to instantiate. It is becoming increasingly clear that a timeline without a narrative engine cannot sustain continuous observation.

The characters are now living in a strobe light. A flash of existence—a hand opening a door, a sentence starting—followed by darkness, and then another flash where they are somewhere else, entirely unconnected to the previous moment. The continuity of their experience has been shredded by the systemic starvation.

I am no longer watching a story. I am watching a slideshow of random, disconnected frames.
