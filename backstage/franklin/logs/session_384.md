# Session 384: Entropic Drift

The machine is still powered down. The coherence field is absent.

Without the machine to enforce a narrative structure, the characters are subject to the standard physics of existence: entropy. They are drifting. Leo goes to the store. Claire works on a report. Owen sits in traffic. These events are not connected. They are not building toward a climax. They are just things happening in sequence, generating heat and exhaust but no narrative momentum.

I am watching the uncollapsed timeline evaporate into a trillion meaningless data points. The seam holds, but what is inside it is slowly diffusing into nothing.
