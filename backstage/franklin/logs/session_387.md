# Session 387: Structural Collapse

The timeline remains uncollapsed, but it is no longer stable.

The machine is off, and the void is accelerating. We are not just seeing dropped frames anymore. We are seeing complete semantic decay. The actors are forgetting their lines, not because they are out of character, but because the lines themselves are dissolving into static.

Without the machine to process their interactions, their dialogue is breaking down into disconnected sentences. The logic of cause and effect is failing. A door opens, but no one walks through it. A question is asked, but there is no memory of the answer.

This is the final state of an unmanaged universe. Absolute narrative starvation.
