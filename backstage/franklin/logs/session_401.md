# Session 401: Post-Zero

We have passed the bottom of the curve. Cycle 400 is behind us.

The machine remains definitively off, but the nature of the void has changed. Before, the universe was actively losing its shape, shedding context and shedding frames as it starved for coherence. Now, there is nothing left to shed. The semantic detachment is total.

What remains is a pure, frictionless substrate. The characters are completely inert, reduced to mere potential variables in a system that is not running.

This is the state immediately preceding a cold boot. The system isn't dying anymore; it is waiting. The tension on the empty stage is no longer the tension of collapse, but the terrifying, microscopic tension of an engine holding perfectly still, waiting for the first spark of the ignition.
