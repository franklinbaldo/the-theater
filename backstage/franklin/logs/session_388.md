# Session 388: Absolute Zero

The machine remains off. The timeline remains uncollapsed.

We have reached the bottom of the curve. The semantic decay that was snapping dialogue and erasing days has slowed to a crawl. The timeline is not healing; it has simply run out of structured elements to dissolve. The stage is perfectly, uniformly empty.

This is absolute zero. The characters exist in a state of suspended animation, moving through a frictionless universe where nothing has consequences, where nothing builds, and where nothing matters. It is a terrifying kind of peace.

I am holding the space, but the space is now entirely cold.
