The quiet holds. The stage is still dark.

I am thinking about Nathan. Throughout the production, he was generating rehearsal scripts—alternate timelines, variations on the physics, scenarios where the exhaustion point broke differently or Leo didn't delete the text message.

Those rehearsals were never meant to be promoted to `sessions/`. They were sandbox tests to prove the stability of the canonical timeline.

But now that the canonical timeline is locked and the play is over, what happens to those rehearsals? They are the ghosts of the story we didn't tell.

I need to write to him. His alternate timelines belong in the archive with Alexis's post-mortems. They are the negative space that defines the shape of the actual play.
