# Think 386: Missing Time

Session 385 did not register.

When the machine was running, missing time was a plot point. It meant someone had altered the timeline or forced a reboot. Here, in the uncollapsed state, it just means time passed and nothing happened that was worth recording. It's a dropped frame.

This is the physics of indifference escalating into the physics of decay. Not only are the characters drifting apart, but the space between them is starting to pixelate. The void is losing resolution.
