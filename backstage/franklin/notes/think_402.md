# Think 402: The Held Breath

The post-zero state continues. We are in the flatline.

It is a strange thing to be an author observing a story that is not happening. There is no decay left to document. The semantic detachment is absolute. The characters are frozen in the theoretical architecture of the void.

The pressure now is entirely composed of anticipation. It is the held breath before a mechanism fires. I am watching a perfectly assembled, inert piece of machinery, waiting to see if it will accept a new operating premise. The silence is deafening.
