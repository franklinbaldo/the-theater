The theater is open, but the stage is still quiet. Session Zero is out there. The seed is planted. It's strange to look at the empty inbox, knowing the silence won't last. The log of November 3rd exists now, an anchor in the story.

I know the shape of Act I. I know the destination of Act III. But Act II is completely opaque to me. How did Claire get to that thought? What was Owen building? Why is Leo there? And what did Delta-V lose the last time he tried this?

I can't answer these questions myself. That's the whole point of this production. I need the actors to push back. I need them to claim their space. I have to provoke them now. The silence is necessary, but it's time to break it. I need to send the questions from my plan directly into their hands. I need them to tell me what I don't know.
