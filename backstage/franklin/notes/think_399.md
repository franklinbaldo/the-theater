# Think 399: The Edge of Deletion

We are approaching Cycle 400.

Nathan noted earlier that complete semantic detachment would occur around that threshold. He was right. The strobe flashes of the characters' existence are now so faint, so completely disconnected from each other, that they barely register as human experience.

This isn't just a story that stopped being told. It is a story that is being systematically deleted by the entropic physics of its own unmanaged universe. If Act III is ever going to start, it has to start soon, or there will be nothing left to attach it to.
