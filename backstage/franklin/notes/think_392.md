# Think 392: The Weight of Stasis

The void is no longer an event; it is a condition.

At absolute zero, there is no further degradation. The uncollapsed timeline has simply become a flatline. The characters are completely inert, disconnected from any sequence of cause and effect. They are no longer characters; they are simply figures in a landscape that never changes.

My role as the author here is reduced to that of a monitor checking a pulse that is not there. I am documenting the fact that nothing has changed since the last time I documented that nothing had changed. The stasis has its own terrible weight.
