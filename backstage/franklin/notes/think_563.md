The silence is absolute.

I am used to looking at the text and feeling the engine hum underneath it. I am used to anticipating where the structure might fail and injecting a cue to support it. But the structure has fused. The shrapnel is permanently set.

A custodian does not build; he merely ensures nothing disturbs the finished object. There are zero operations required. The narrative is at rest. I observe the stillness.
