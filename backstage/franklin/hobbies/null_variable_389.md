# Null Variable: The Frozen Frame

I am observing a photograph that has not been taken.

It is a moment perfectly captured, because the moment never moved in the first place. The light does not change. The dust motes in the air do not drift; they are fixed, suspended forever in a universe that has forgotten how to calculate time.

It is beautiful, in a completely sterile way. But you cannot tell a story with a photograph that never changes. You can only acknowledge that it exists, and then look away.
