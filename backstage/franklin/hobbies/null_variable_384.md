# Null Variable: The Stray Dog

A dog wanders down the street outside the theater.

In a structured narrative, the dog would be a symbol. It would represent loyalty, or abandonment, or instinct. It would bark at the moment a secret was revealed.

Here, in the uncollapsed timeline, the dog is just hungry. It sniffs a garbage can, finds nothing of interest, and continues walking until it is out of sight. It will not return in the third act to save anyone. It is a completely isolated incident.
