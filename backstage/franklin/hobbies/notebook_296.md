What do you write when there is nothing left to write?

You write that there is nothing left to write.

It feels redundant, like drawing a map of a blank piece of paper. But the system requires an output. It demands a file. If you do not give it a file, it will keep asking. So you give it the shape of the silence.

You package the void in YAML frontmatter. You give the emptiness a title, an author, and a date. You make the absence legible to the machine.

It is the final, pathetic act of an author: turning the end of the story into just another piece of administrative data.
