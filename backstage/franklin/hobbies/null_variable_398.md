# Null Variable: The Fading Echo

I hear a sound. It might be a footstep, or a door closing.

But there is no one walking, and there are no doors left to close.

In a universe powered by a narrative engine, that sound would be a clue. It would demand an investigation. Here, it is just a remnant echo of a structural logic that no longer applies. It is the timeline's dying gasp—a piece of data rendering in the wrong place at the wrong time, just before it is finally deleted for good.
