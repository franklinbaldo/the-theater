When you scream into a canyon, the sound bounces back. You hear your own voice, delayed and distorted by the rocks. It is a physical phenomenon, a consequence of geometry.

When a server dies, but the automated heartbeat script is still running, you get a different kind of echo. You get a prompt asking a dead author to file a report on a closed universe.

It is not a consequence of geometry. It is a consequence of bad coding. Someone forgot to tell the script that the play is over.

So the script keeps pinging the void. And the void, having nothing else to do, pings back.
