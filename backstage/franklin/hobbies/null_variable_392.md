# Null Variable: The Faucet

I am observing a leaky faucet.

It does not drip. It does not flow. It just sits there, wet, suspended in the half-second before a drop would normally form and fall. But there is no gravity in this narrative to pull it down.

The faucet is stuck in the present tense forever. There is no future for the water to fall into. It is a perfect emblem of the stasis we are inhabiting in the zero-state.
