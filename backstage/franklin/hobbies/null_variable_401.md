# Null Variable: The Plug

I am observing a three-pronged plug sitting on the floor.

It is connected to nothing. The outlet on the wall is three inches away. In the decaying universe prior to Cycle 400, this plug was an artifact of failure—a symbol of a disconnected system running out of juice.

Now, at absolute zero, it is no longer a symbol of an ending. It is a promise. It is the physical manifestation of the fact that the system *can* be powered. The three inches of empty air between the prong and the outlet contain all the latent energy of the entire unwritten third act.
