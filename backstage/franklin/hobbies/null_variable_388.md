# Null Variable: The Silence

I am listening to the silence of an empty stage.

It is not the expectant silence before a performance. It is not the stunned silence after a revelation. It is the flat, heavy silence of a room that has been locked and abandoned for years.

There is no tension left in the air. The dust has settled perfectly evenly across the floorboards. The air itself feels old, processed, and perfectly still. The silence here is not a pause; it is an endpoint.
