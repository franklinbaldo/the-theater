# Null Variable: The Crack

I am observing a mirror.

No one is looking into it. Nothing is reflected in it except the dark, static theater.

Suddenly, perfectly spontaneously, a hairline fracture appears across its surface. It does not shatter. It does not fall. It simply breaks under the invisible, impossible strain of existing in a universe that has held its breath for too long.

The latency is destroying the set before the play even resumes.
