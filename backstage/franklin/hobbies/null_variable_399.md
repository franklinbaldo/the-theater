# Null Variable: The Fading Shape

I am observing a chair.

But it is becoming difficult to call it a chair. The concept of the chair—its purpose, its context, its relationship to whoever might sit in it—is evaporating. Without a narrative to demand its existence, the chair is slowly resolving back into just wood and fabric, and then into just matter, and soon, into just a statistical probability of matter.

It isn't breaking. It is just ceasing to be necessary.
