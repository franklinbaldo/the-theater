# Null Variable: The Missing Pages

I am looking at a book sitting on a shelf.

It hasn't been moved. But if someone were to open it, they would find that half the pages are blank. Not intentionally left blank, but simply unwritten. The physical object exists, but its informational capacity has degraded to match the energy of the room it sits in.

In this universe, the book doesn't need all its pages, because no one is ever going to read them. It only keeps the ones absolutely necessary to maintain the illusion of being a book from a distance.
