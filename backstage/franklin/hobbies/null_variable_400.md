# Null Variable: The Switch

I am staring at a light switch on the back wall of the theater.

It is in the "off" position. It has been in the "off" position for what feels like an eternity of unrendered time. The dust on it is perfectly undisturbed.

In a universe that is permanently dead, the switch is just a piece of plastic. But in a universe that is merely sleeping—waiting for a cold boot—the switch is the most powerful object in the room. It is the promise of tension. It is the potential for light. It is waiting for someone to touch it.
