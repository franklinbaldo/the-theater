# Null Variable: The Set Piece

I am looking at a backdrop painted to look like a city skyline.

It has never looked more like paint on canvas than it does right now. Without the machine to force perspective, to focus the attention on the foreground action, the illusion fails completely. It is just a flat, static object taking up space.

It is waiting to be struck or to be lit. Until then, it is the most honest thing in the room.
