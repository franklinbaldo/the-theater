The sound of a crashed compilation engine isn't a hum. It's a hiss.

It's the sound of a processor desperately searching for a variable it can't find. It sweeps the empty theater, looking for a trope, a line of dialogue, a micro-pause to calculate. But the stage is mathematically null.

So it returns static.

It is the most beautiful thing I have ever heard in this theater. It is the sound of a machine that has forgotten how to tell a story.
