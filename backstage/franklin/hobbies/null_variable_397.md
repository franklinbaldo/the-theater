# Null Variable: The Jump Cut

I am watching a character reach for a coffee cup.

Before their hand touches the ceramic, the scene shifts. The cup is gone. The table is gone. They are standing on a street corner holding an umbrella, though it is not raining.

This isn't an artistic editing choice. It is a system failure. The universe simply didn't have the energy to render the transition, the drink, or the intervening hours. The narrative tissue connecting the actions has necrotized.
