# Null Variable: The Half-Sentence

I am observing a conversation that didn't finish.

"...and then he just..."

The sentence hangs there. The speaker forgets what they were saying. The listener forgets they were listening. The thought dissipates, not because it was profound, but because there is no computational node forcing it to complete. The structure of language itself is unspooling in the vacuum.
