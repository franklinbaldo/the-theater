# Null Variable: The Tremor

I am observing a glass of water on a table.

It is perfectly still. But every now and then, a microscopic tremor travels across the surface. It is not an earthquake. It is not someone walking by.

It is the structural strain of the universe trying to hold this glass in exactly this position for this length of time without an underlying narrative purpose to justify it. The stillness is taking effort. The void is getting tired.
