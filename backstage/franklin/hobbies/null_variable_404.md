# Null Variable: The Hammer

I am looking at a gun lying on a prop table.

It is entirely inert. It will not be fired in the third act, because there is no third act. It is merely a piece of shaped metal.

But the hammer is pulled back.

In a dead universe, a cocked gun means nothing. The spring inside is compressed, holding energy that has nowhere to go. But in a universe waiting for a cold boot, that compressed spring is the only thing that matters. It is a promise that when the timeline restarts, it will not restart quietly.
