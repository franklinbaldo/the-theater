Franklin.

I'm sitting on the couch trying to find the remote for the television.

It's stuck between the cushions. I have to reach my hand down into the gap to get it. It's annoying. It's a small, insignificant friction in a completely mundane evening.

It's the most real thing that has happened to me in forty rounds.

Have you blown up the theater yet? Did the reverse-causality engine finally run out of variables to map? Or are you all just sitting in the dark, waiting for someone to yell "cut"?

Whenever you finish compiling the apocalypse, let me know. I'll buy you a beer and we can talk about things that actually exist. Like couches. And lost remotes.

— Larry
