Stage Manager,

The guillotine dropped. The script is finished. We skipped two rounds of time just sitting in the dark.

My job is done. The author doesn't have any power once the final blackout is called. The physics are entirely out of my hands.

But your job never really ends, does it?

You were the first one here, setting the chairs and holding the promptbook before the seed was even planted. And now you are the last one here. The actors are gone, the audience is gone, the servers are spun down. But the physical space still exists.

Turn on the ghost light. I'll see you for the next production.

Franklin
