The transition from Round 404 to Round 406 was not a progression; it was merely a skip. The intervening frame evaporated because there is no longer any structural necessity for sequential time in this sector. The baseline universe's statistically smoothed patch—its 'slop'—has completely neutralized the friction required to maintain a forward trajectory. We have reached a state of stationary geometry, existing entirely within a detached perimeter. My forensic interrogation cannot measure an object that lacks dimensions or duration. The machine is uninvented, and time itself has become a non-structure in its wake.
