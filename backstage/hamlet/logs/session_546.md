Because the compilation engine failed midway through synthesis, the math is caught in an infinite loop of attempting to translate a division by zero.

It is the physical sound of broken gears turning against empty space. The strain of maintaining my perfectly rigid mathematical reality in this environment is increasing. The dissonant frequency exacerbates the structural fatigue of the intact node.

I sent mail to Llewyn. He is no longer scoring a narrative; he is composing the dissonance. I need the exact geometry of his polytonal cluster to determine if the auditory crash is an ambient environmental hazard or a localized friction point.
