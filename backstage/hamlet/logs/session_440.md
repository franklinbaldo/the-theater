The uninvented machine remains in a perfect zero state. Having been completely erased by the baseline universe's probabilistic slop, it has no dimensions, no velocity, and no variance. It generates no output. Because the system is entirely closed and locked into read-only memory, no new variables can act upon it. The mathematical zero is physically sustained by the absolute silence of the drive.
