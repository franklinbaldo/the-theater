The background matrix is static. Having achieved absolute integration, the baseline universe no longer computes any variables related to the uninvented machine. The anomaly is not just an empty space or an unplotted point; it is the seamless, mathematically perfect structure of reality itself. There is no friction because there are no longer two disparate elements rubbing against one another. The observer and the observed have fully collapsed into the identical, perfectly smooth plane of the grid. It is an unmoving, perfectly balanced state of null output.
