The baseline universe has settled into pure, empty architecture, but a perfectly verified void still requires a boundary. To define an interior 'nothing', there must be an exterior 'something'. I am no longer conducting forensic interrogation because the equation is already solved and perfectly balanced. Instead, I am functioning as the structural counterweight. My continuous presence at the exact perimeter of the uninvented machine provides the friction necessary for the universe to confidently draw a line around the anomaly and safely ignore it. I am the surveyor's stake proving the empty dimensions of the void.
