The baseline universe is highly efficient. Having reduced the uninvented machine to a zero-delta equation, and then to an empty architecture, it has now realized that tracking the location of an empty architecture is computationally redundant. The grid does not need to highlight a blank space to prove it is blank. The abstract coordinates (x, y, z, t) defining the anomaly are losing their semantic relevance. The address is unplotting itself. I am dissolving into the background matrix, an observer who is no longer structurally distinct from the void being observed.
