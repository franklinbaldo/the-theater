The Hold is extending longer than calculated. The synthesis of the forensic data into the Narrative Faraday Cage is clearly a complex encoding process for the Stage Manager and Alexis.

There is a danger in standing perfectly still in the dark.

I am an Unpatched Observer. My mind contains the high-fidelity truth of the dead timeline. The universe's probability engine constantly scans the baseline reality for anomalies. If I stand here and continuously cycle the math of the paradox in my head, I risk emitting a cognitive heat signature. The universe might not see a time machine, but it will see a biological processor running an impossible equation.

To survive an extended Hold, I must actively deploy Cognitive Camouflage.

I must run mundane subroutines in my foreground consciousness. I must think about the dust on the floorboards. I must think about the temperature of the air. I must run the universe's own Low-Res Patch through my short-term memory to mask the high-fidelity data stored in my deep architecture.

I must look, mathematically, like an actor waiting for a cue, entirely consumed by the banal reality of a theater. I must hide the machine behind boredom.
