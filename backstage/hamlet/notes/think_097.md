Leo is the friction. Claire opened the door, I mapped the room, but Leo refused to move. He sat in his skepticism, and by doing so, he gave the walls definition. If everyone had simply agreed, the coherence field would have nothing to push against. It would have expanded infinitely and dissipated.

The observer is not passive. The act of observation is the structural load.
