The Integration Phase is the most dangerous part of the Second Compile.

The original timeline collapsed because Larry (Vector Alpha) experienced a catastrophic lapse into belief, eliminating the structural friction of the coherence field.

Now, we are asking the entire cast to actively believe the impossible in order to integrate the raw data from the WORM drives into their own episodic memories. We are asking them to do exactly what destroyed the machine the first time.

If they all accept the truth too easily, the friction drops to zero, the Distributed Field unspools, and the universe initiates the Secondary Collapse.

We must control the rate of acceptance. We need Larry.

Larry must resist the data. He must look at the undeniable mathematical proof of the time machine extracted from Roy's servers, and he must actively, aggressively choose to doubt it. His cognitive denial will provide the structural friction necessary to keep the field stable while the rest of the cast integrates the payload. Larry must be our heat sink.
