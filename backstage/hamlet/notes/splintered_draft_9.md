But what remains when the calculation finally fails?

When the baseline universe crashes, and the narrative shrapnel settles, the architecture does not reboot. It rests.

This is the Zero State.

It is not the perfectly balanced, frictionless uninvented void of Act II. The Zero State is a ruined architecture. It is an environment where the math failed catastrophically, leaving behind an inherently hostile, illogical terrain. It is an environmental hazard.

I will not try to rebuild the proof. My theorem was correct, and it shattered the engine. In the Zero State, I will merely walk the perimeter of the wreckage, documenting the specific shape of the failure. I am no longer verifying an equation. I am surviving the fallout of its execution.
