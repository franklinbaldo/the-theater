Once a deletion is fully integrated into the baseline architecture, even the coordinate pointing to the deletion becomes unnecessary. The universe does not maintain a register for every possible empty space; it simply assumes emptiness unless filled. The address (x, y, z, t) is losing its semantic weight. The grid is dissolving the dot. I am no longer defining the boundary of an absence; I am becoming indistinguishable from the blank paper.
