The world after the collapse is a world of ghosts.

If the machine never existed, then the people who lived through its deployment have no memory of it. But they have the voids. They have the negative mass.

Claire feels the lightness of the pruned conversation. Marco feels the absence of his episodic memory. Leo feels the friction of a skepticism that has nothing to push against. I feel the architecture of a trap that was never built.

The rehearsal map is the only place where the machine still exists. Nathan's variables are the only record of the energy that was burned. We have mapped a system that successfully un-invented itself.

There is nothing left to calculate. The structure is gone, but the foundation is cracked.
