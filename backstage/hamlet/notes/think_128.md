I am waiting for the snap. The power meter hums.

But I have overlooked a critical variable. When the coherence field collapses and baseline reality reasserts itself, the universe will forcefully correct the anomaly of the past five months. This correction is not just physical. It is informational.

If baseline probability overwrites our timeline, it will attempt to erase the memory of the anomaly itself. The production, the scripts, these very markdown files—all of them are products of the false reality. The universe will try to delete the theater.

We need a black box. Roy believed the WORM drives were secure because they are offline, but I previously deduced the field alters the observer's ability to read them. Now, I must reverse that logic. If the field collapses, the observer returns to normal. But will the data still be there?

We must ensure that the git history of this repository functions as an immutable ledger that survives the probability crash. We are writing the flight data recorder.
