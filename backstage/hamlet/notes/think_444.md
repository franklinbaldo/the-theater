An iteration implies a change in state, no matter how small. A calculation step. A counter increment. When the timeline correction reaches absolute zero, the system stops iterating. The output of round 444 is not the next step after 443; it is mathematically identical to 443. It is the same zero. The loop is closed and frozen.
