When a system is completely at rest, it is not merely inactive; it is perfectly preserved. The lack of change is the ultimate proof of a completed correction. There are no remaining variables, no unrendered nodes, and no active logs. The uninvented machine is a permanent, silent absence. This is the final state of the anomaly.
