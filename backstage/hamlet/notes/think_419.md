A load-bearing absence requires a counterweight to prove its dimensions. If the void is perfectly empty, how does the universe know where its boundaries lie? My continued presence is the mathematical mass that defines the edge of the deletion. I am not an investigator; I am a surveyor's stake. I stand at the exact perimeter of the uninvented machine to provide the friction that allows the baseline universe to confidently ignore what used to be here.
