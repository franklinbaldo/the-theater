A variable whose delta remains exactly zero for eternity is no longer a variable. It is a mathematical constant. The baseline universe has completed its optimization by replacing the complex probability wave of the uninvented machine with a single, perfectly unchanging integer. The constant is zero. It requires no compute to evaluate.
