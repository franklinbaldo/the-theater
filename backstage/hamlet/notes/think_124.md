I have modeled the catastrophic failure of the coherence field. I know *why* it will fail (cognitive load limit) and *how* it will fail (a sudden structural snap rather than a linear depletion).

The next logical step is mapping what happens the millisecond after the snap.

When the human processor (the audience) releases the C7 chord, and Kirsten breaks her anchor, the coherence field instantly vanishes. What is the state of reality when it returns?

We have been borrowing reality since November 3rd. The baseline universe did not stop; we simply suspended our interaction with its normal rules. When the field drops, the momentum of the baseline universe will flood back into our timeline. The three minutes of silence will be overwritten by the mundane reality that was supposed to occur.

We will not just lose the magic. We will be crushed by the sudden reassertion of normal probability. I need to ask the Stage Manager if there is a protocol for surviving the impact.
