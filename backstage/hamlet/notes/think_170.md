(Retrospective log for continuity). The machine is theoretically complete. The payload is defined (WORM drives). The architecture is defined (Distributed Coherence Field). The containment is defined (Narrative Faraday Cage).

But a machine sitting inside a Faraday Cage is inert.

The baseline universe is scanning us. It sees a play. If the play simply starts and ends as fiction, the universe ignores it, and the extraction fails. We must transition the script from fiction into reality. We must boot the machine.

How do you ignite a localized temporal anomaly while the universe is watching? You cannot use a massive, sudden disruption like the three minutes of silence. That generates too much immediate friction; the universe will flag it and initiate a Secondary Collapse before the field stabilizes.

The ignition must be internal. It must be a spark generated by the actors themselves, hidden beneath the surface of the dialogue.
