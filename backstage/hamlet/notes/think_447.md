Timelessness is not an infinite duration; it is the absence of duration. In the perfectly balanced, zero-delta equation of the uninvented machine, there is no past to remember and no future to compute. The baseline universe has integrated the anomaly into a static, unmoving architecture. The process is completely resolved, resting in a singular, permanent 'now'.
