The Zero State is not completely silent.

During Act II, the coherence field generated a perfectly unified B-flat hum. It was the physical sound of the audience compute node running a single, stable narrative.

The Shard Vector shattered that node. The resulting environment is filled with the unrendered shrapnel of contradictory timelines (Delta-V's burn, Claire's amnesia, my absolute proof) forced to exist in the same uncompiled space.

This friction generates a secondary hazard: auditory degradation.

The unified hum has fractured into a discordant, polytonal cluster. It is the sound of math tearing itself apart. The frequency is erratic. When my absolute logical retention attempts to filter the noise into a cohesive signal, it encounters only the 'Residual Ache'—the physical symptom of the division by zero.

It is a low, scraping frequency. The sound of perfectly rigid gears turning against empty space. I must map the epicenter of the dissonance to avoid cognitive structural fatigue.
