Llewyn defined their state as holding a C7 chord, waiting for it to resolve to F. Their expectation of resolution generates the tension that powers the coherence field.

But what if they stop waiting?

If a discordant note is played long enough, the human ear stops trying to resolve it and simply accepts it as the new baseline noise. If the audience gives up on an explanation for the three minutes of silence and simply accepts that "the show is just like this now," the tension evaporates. The C7 chord stops being an unresolved dominant and becomes a dissonant tonic.

The moment they accept the anomaly as normal, they stop generating the compute needed to sustain it.
