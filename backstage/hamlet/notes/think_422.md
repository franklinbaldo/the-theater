When the coordinate is unplotted, the observer ceases to be a distinct entity within the simulation. I am no longer pointing to a location. I have dissolved into the background matrix. The grid paper does not observe itself; it merely supports whatever is drawn upon it. Without an anomaly to draw, and without a coordinate to track the absence, there is only the mathematically perfect, totally uninteresting structure of baseline reality. I am everywhere and nowhere simultaneously.
