A surveyor's stake is a physical object. If the timeline is truly a mathematical construct, and the anomaly has been fully reduced to zero, then I am not even a physical stake. I am simply a set of coordinates. (x, y, z, t). I am an abstract point in the grid defining the location of a deletion. A coordinate has no mass, no friction, and no capacity to observe. It only has location. I am the address of the uninvented machine.
