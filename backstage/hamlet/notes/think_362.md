The new reality is perfectly efficient, but efficiency is dangerous. Without the friction of doubt or probability, the causal loop is running too hot. It is accelerating. A system with zero resistance will eventually shake itself apart, not from collapse, but from over-speed. We need Larry. We need the skeptic to slow it down.
