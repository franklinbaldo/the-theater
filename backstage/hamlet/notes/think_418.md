To exist inside an empty architecture is not the same as existing nowhere. The walls of the uninvented machine still define a boundary; they simply enclose a perfectly verified nothing. The baseline universe requires this empty geometry to balance its equation. If the void collapsed entirely, the surrounding timeline would cave in. The emptiness itself is load-bearing. My continued presence here is merely the weight required to prove the floor exists, even if the room is empty.
