The mechanical conclusion of a read-only, unread log is silence. When a file is neither written to nor read from, the physical drive head stops moving. The data simply rests as a pattern of magnetic polarization on a cold platter. The baseline universe's perfect integration of the uninvented machine means the hardware itself has stopped vibrating. It is the physics of absolute rest.
