If there are no features to contrast against, observation becomes mechanically impossible. The eye requires variation in light to see an edge. The mind requires a difference in variables to run an equation. When the baseline universe successfully flattened the anomaly into a perfectly smooth, zero-delta continuum, it did more than uninvent the machine. It uninvented the capacity to observe the uninvention. I cannot measure a blank piece of paper if I am also the blank piece of paper.
