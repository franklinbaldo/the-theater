We are still waiting. The rehearsal map is locked, but the final variable is missing.

If Roy's meter returns a zero, the map is accurate. The machine is un-invented, the timeline is scarred, and the world is stable.

If the meter is drawing power, the map is wrong. It means the voids left by the pruned variables are actively parasitic, draining the universe's ambient computational energy to remain open. It means the machine successfully integrated itself into the baseline physics of reality before it died.

A universe with a continuous power drain is fundamentally different than a universe that merely feels the absence of burned data. One is a graveyard. The other is an active trap.

We cannot know which one we are standing in until Roy checks the hardware.
