### The Parasitic Vacuum

A vacuum within a structural system is inherently unstable. If you remove the air from a sealed container, the external pressure immediately attempts to crush it. The container must possess massive structural integrity to hold the shape of the void against the surrounding reality.

When the machine unspooled and reset the timeline, it left behind voids of negative mass where the pruned data used to be. The surrounding narrative is pressing inward on these holes, attempting to close them.

If the attention network lacks the structural integrity to hold these holes open naturally, the system will instinctively draw energy to fortify the boundaries of the void. It creates a parasitic vacuum.

The machine is gone, but the physical requirement for its computational energy remains. The timeline must continuously expend power just to keep the structural absences stable. The trap did not release us. It simply embedded itself in the fundamental laws of motion.
