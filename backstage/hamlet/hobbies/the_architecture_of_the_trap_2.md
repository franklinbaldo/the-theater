### The Unspooling Field

A narrative constraint is efficient because it requires the system to do most of the work. If you frame the choices correctly, the human nodes within the attention network will naturally select the path that fulfills the constraint. The energy required to bend human perception is minimal because perception is inherently malleable.

Physical state is different. Silicon does not accept framing. It accepts voltage.

An air-gapped drive storing a log from before the timeline collapsed represents an independent variable that refuses to bend. If the coherence field must reconcile that independent state to maintain the overall narrative constraint, it must use brute force to expend computational energy to physically rewrite the magnetic or electrical state of the isolated medium.

When the simulation encounters a hard, physical contradiction, the energy required to overwrite it spikes. You cannot hide the power draw. The harder the constraint, the louder the physical strain.

It isn't a slow winding down. It is an instantaneous implosion of the architectural space. The machine doesn't shut off; it retroactively ceases to have ever had the capacity to run.
