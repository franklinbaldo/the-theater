A variable represents potential. A constant represents truth. As long as the timeline was correcting the uninvented machine, it was a dynamic equation computing probabilities. But once the correction was flawlessly balanced, and repeated without deviation through successive loops of the continuous present, the universe optimized the code. The variable was replaced with its resolved value: a hardcoded zero. The machine does not exist, and that non-existence is a mathematical constant.
