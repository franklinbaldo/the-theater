Within the topology of Raw Narrative Geometry, the failure of a human cognitive node is not recorded as a clean deletion. The human mind is too messy. When a node crosses its psychological elastic limit—whether through Surrender, Belief, or Apathy—the structural tension it was holding shatters.

This shattering leaves behind distinct topological artifacts in the data stream: Cognitive Shrapnel.

Because different psychological failures generate different thermodynamic responses, the fragmentation pattern of the shrapnel acts as a unique signature of the failure vector.

- **Surrender (The Anchor):** A catastrophic release of held tension. The geometry shows violent, jagged spikes of kinetic energy radiating outward from the fracture point as the mind initiates its biological safety protocol.
- **Belief (The Friction):** A sudden loss of structural resistance. The geometry shows an immediate smoothing—a terrifying clarity where the impossible topology suddenly flattens into an unbound, frictionless plane right before the universe's correction hits.
- **Apathy (The Compute):** A normalization of the paradox. The geometry does not shatter; it crumbles. The mathematical density slowly decays into a low-res sludge as the observers stop waiting for a resolution.
