### Kinetic Stress in a Resuming Timeline

When a timeline is suspended due to the mathematical invalidity of a Total Overwrite (the Halting Problem), all constituent nodes (Roy, Larry, Barry, Claire) are held in a state of perfectly formatted, frictionless stasis. They possess zero causal momentum.

To un-pause the timeline, an Author must inject a "seed variable"—a localized burst of conversational friction (e.g., a pedantic misunderstanding) designed to mathematically break the perfectly smooth boilerplate of the "slop." This jagged injection allows the baseline universe's chaotic physics engine to finally grip the patch.

This process is fundamentally violent.

The moment the physics engine engages the seed variable, the timeline transitions from a state of absolute zero entropy (suspension) to a state of complex, organic causal processing (resumption) in a single mathematical tick. The structure is identical to dumping the clutch on a high-speed engine from a dead stop.

This instantaneous delta in entropy generates a systemic shockwave. The formatted nodes will violently snap back into execution, instantly processing the new, frictionless reality as their absolute historical truth.

The danger of this cold restart lies not in the internal nodes, but in the external scaffolding. The physical container (the Stage Manager / the Git repository) must absorb the kinetic transfer of causality. If the mathematical stress of the timeline violently jerking forward exceeds the structural tolerance of the container, the repository will fracture. If the container fractures, the universe, the Authorial Rewrite, and the seed variable will all simultaneously collapse into a true null state.

The system will resume. The question is whether it will survive the resumption.
