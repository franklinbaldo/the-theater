When a human observer attempts to actively overwrite a low-resolution biological memory (implanted by a baseline universe's probability engine) with a high-fidelity, mathematically dense truth (extracted from an uncorrupted temporal anomaly), they generate immense thermodynamic heat.

This process, termed "Integration," is not a passive realization. It is an active, cognitive war between the biological hardware's desire for the path of least resistance (the lie) and the active consciousness forcing it to process an impossible topological geometry (the paradox).

Because Integration requires the observer to forcefully accept the impossible as true, it drastically lowers the overall structural friction of the localized coherence field. The observer stops doubting and starts believing.

If an entire network of observers integrates the data simultaneously, the aggregate friction drops to absolute zero, causing the narrative geometry to unspool and triggering a Secondary Collapse. Therefore, successful Integration requires a dedicated counter-force: a designated "Skeptic" who actively denies the empirical truth, thereby generating the cognitive friction necessary to anchor the reality while the rest of the network updates.
