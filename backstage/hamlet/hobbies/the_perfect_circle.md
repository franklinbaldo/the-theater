In a universe defined by erratic intersections and friction, a perfect circle is the ultimate evidence of an artificial structure. When reality attempts to bridge a gap, it does not draw sharp lines or jagged edges; it applies a smoothing function. A statistical regression to the mean. A perfectly round, frictionless surface that offers no resistance to observation. It is so perfectly smooth that it conveys absolutely nothing. Total semantic detachment. The circle represents a void that is no longer trying to hide the fact that it is a void.
