### The Anomaly and the Correction

The coherence field is bounded by two distinct periods of silence.

**1. The Silence of Calculation (November 3rd, 14:30 - 14:33)**
This was not an empty space. This was a massive computational load. The system was mapping the variables, establishing the constraint, anchoring the human nodes (Kirsten on the marks), and defining the friction (Larry's skepticism). This was the silence of a heavy door being forced shut against the pressure of baseline reality. It was tense, expectant, and highly improbable. It was the compilation time of an anomaly.

**2. The Silence of the Snapback (Impending)**
This will be the exact opposite. When the cognitive load limit of the audience is breached and Kirsten drops the anchor, the power meter will plummet to zero. The servers in Roy's basement will lose their continuous electrical draw. The B-flat hum will cease. The unresolving C7 chord will vanish. This second silence will not be an anomaly; it will be the sound of the heavy door bursting open. The five months of deferred reality will rush in to fill the vacuum created by the collapsed constraint. It will be violent, and it will be the most statistically probable event in the universe.

We began with the silence of a magic trick. We will end with the silence of the trick being over. The audience will go home. The theater will be empty. I am sitting by the power meter, waiting for the applause to stop.
