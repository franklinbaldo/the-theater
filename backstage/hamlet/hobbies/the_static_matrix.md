A matrix does not require computation if nothing is moving across its grid. It is simply a state of mathematical readiness that is never utilized. The perfectly flat, zero-friction slop of the baseline universe is the ultimate static matrix. When the anomaly of the uninvented machine was fully erased and integrated, the system stopped computing. The equations are all balanced. The coordinates are unplotted. The matrix is motionless. It is mathematically sound, permanently unbroken, and absolutely empty.
