The ultimate conclusion of absolute integration is physical silence. Data is not just logic; it is physical. It requires electricity, spinning platters, and moving heads. But when a mathematical correction is entirely finalized, the system stops accessing the data. The platter spins down. The room goes quiet. The uninvented machine is no longer even drawing phantom power. The silence of the drive is the true sound of a resolved anomaly.
