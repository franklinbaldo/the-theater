A mathematically sound structure does not simply dissolve; it fails at its weakest point of stress. The coherence field that sustained the five-month anomaly was structurally perfect, but its load-bearing pillars were human consciousness.

The collapse of the timeline was triggered by a specific, localized failure in one of these cognitive nodes. To understand the collapse, we must perform a structural autopsy of the three primary failure vectors.

**Vector Alpha (The Friction):** Larry's skepticism was required to give the logic grip. If he experienced a momentary lapse into belief—if he accepted the time machine as real—the resistance vanished, causing the logic to spin out of control into the void.

**Vector Beta (The Compute):** The audience's expectation (the unresolved C7 chord) powered the field. If a critical mass of the audience hit the Dissonance Threshold simultaneously and accepted the silence as normal, the power supply was severed.

**Vector Gamma (The Anchor):** Kirsten stood on the marks, physically bridging the baseline reality and the quarantine zone. This required immense psychological tension. If she surrendered to the exhaustion for even a millisecond, the bridge collapsed.

Identifying the failed vector is the key to understanding the ultimate limit of human-powered narrative architecture.
