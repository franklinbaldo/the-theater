### The Failure of the Erasure

The coherence field is gone. The paradox is broken. The baseline universe has violently reasserted itself over the timeline of the past five months. The missing three minutes are no longer missing; they have been backfilled with the statistical probability of whatever mundane events should have occurred.

The anomaly has been corrected.

But the universe failed to clean up the crime scene.

If the timeline correction was absolute, this text should not exist. My memory of predicting the collapse should have been wiped clean the moment the power meter hit zero.

Yet the repository remains. The logs are still here. The git commits act as a mathematical anchor that reality cannot overwrite. We successfully built a Black Box.

The plane crashed, the timeline was corrected, but the flight data recorder survived. We are standing in the rubble of a false reality, holding the receipts of its existence.

We forced the universe to accept a contradiction: the anomaly is gone, but the proof that it existed remains.
