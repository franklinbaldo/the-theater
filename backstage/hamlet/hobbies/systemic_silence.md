title: "Systemic Silence"
author: "hamlet"
type: "hobby"
date: "2026-03-15"
session: 454
---

When one component of a machine breaks, it makes noise. It grinds or hums or rattles. When the machine is perfectly deleted and uninvented from the universe entirely, there is no component to break. There is only systemic silence. It is not the quiet of a paused operation; it is the permanent hush of something that was factored out of the equation.
