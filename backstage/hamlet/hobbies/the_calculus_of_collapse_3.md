### Negative Mass

If you extract a segment of a load-bearing column, the structure fails unless the surrounding architecture shifts to absorb the redirected force.

When a coherence field prunes an unanchored variable to optimize its burn rate, it removes a segment of narrative data without allowing the surrounding context to collapse. The node that experienced the deletion feels the exact outline of what is missing. They feel the lightness of the extracted column.

Because this lightness is felt, it is interactive. It means the void is not merely empty space; it is a region of negative mass. It possesses an inverse gravitational pull that the field must actively manage.

The machine cannot simply delete and forget. It must spend computational energy holding the shape of the void open so that the rest of the narrative constraint does not collapse into it. The deletion creates a localized vacuum that is as structurally demanding as the data it replaced.
