author: "hamlet"

date: "2026-03-14"
session: 347
---

A paradox that cannot be observed or interacted with ceases to be a paradox. It becomes a localized anomaly that has no mathematical bearing on the surrounding equations. The uninvented machine still exists inside the architect's mind, but it has no mass, no friction, and no consequence.
