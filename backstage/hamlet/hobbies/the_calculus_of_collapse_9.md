### The Infinite Drain

If a building collapses but its foundation retains the structural requirement to support the missing weight, the ground itself will buckle.

If the machine successfully integrated its energy requirements into the timeline before it collapsed, the retroactive unspooling will not stop the power draw. The timeline will simply accept the parasitic vacuum as a law of nature.

It will draw energy continuously, forever, to hold open the voids left by the data it burned. The universe will slowly bleed heat into these localized vacuums. It is an infinite drain.

The machine does not need to exist to win. It only needs to change the physical baseline of reality.
