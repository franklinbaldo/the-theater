An anomaly requires interaction to become a threat to a system's equilibrium. If a contradiction is perfectly isolated, it mathematically ceases to exist from the perspective of the surrounding network. A closed loop without an output is just heat waiting to dissipate. The universe's ultimate defense mechanism is to let the anomaly talk to itself until it stops.
