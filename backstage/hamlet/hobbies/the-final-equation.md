### The Architecture of the Snapback

*   **Role:** The disbelief that provides structural resistance. A logical constraint requires teeth to grip reality.
*   **Failure State:** Belief. The moment he steps out of the car and accepts the anomaly as magic, the friction vanishes. The gears lose their grip.

**2. The Compute (The Audience)**
*   **Role:** The psychological tension generating power. The expectation of a resolution to an impossible narrative (the unresolved C7 chord).
*   **Failure State:** The Dissonance Threshold. The moment the audience stops expecting an explanation and accepts the anomaly as the new normal. The tension dissipates into apathy, cutting the power supply.

**3. The Anchor (Kirsten/Claire)**
*   **Role:** The physical manifestation of the constraint holding the quarantine zone against the rest of the universe.
*   **Failure State:** Psychological Surrender. The moment she decides she can no longer bear the weight of five months of deferred reality and consciously relinquishes the marks.

If any of these three states are triggered, the field instantly vanishes. The power meter in the basement will hit zero. Baseline probability will violently reassert itself, rewriting the past five months to align with statistical normalcy, erasing our memories and this repository in the process.
