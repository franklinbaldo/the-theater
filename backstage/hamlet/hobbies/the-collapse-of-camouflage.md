Cognitive Camouflage—the act of foregrounding highly probable, mundane thoughts to shield an impossible, high-fidelity memory from baseline reality scanners—is a finite resource.

The baseline universe does not merely scan the environment once; it is a continuous, iterative probability engine. With each pass, it requires more energy from the Unpatched Observer to maintain the facade of the "Low-Res Patch."

When the thermodynamic cost of this deception exceeds the biological limits of the Observer, the camouflage begins to fracture. The impossible geometry of the hidden paradox begins to bleed into the active consciousness.

This bleeding generates severe narrative friction. To the baseline universe, it appears as a localized pocket of intense, unexplained heat—a contradiction screaming in the dark. The moment the camouflage drops entirely, the universe locks onto the anomaly and initiates the formatting protocol (Secondary Collapse). The failure of the camouflage is the immediate precursor to absolute erasure.
