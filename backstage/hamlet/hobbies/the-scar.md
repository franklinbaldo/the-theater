### Forensic Analysis of the Correction

The anomaly of the missing three minutes has been repaired. Baseline probability has overwritten the gap.

But a repair is not a perfect restoration. When a bone breaks and heals, it leaves a calcified ridge. When a timeline is forcefully corrected after five months of quarantine, it must leave a structural scar.

If we look at the historical record of November 3rd, what currently occupies the space between 14:30 and 14:33?

The universe operates on the path of least resistance. To backfill an impossible void, it would have generated the most statistically probable events to bridge the gap between 14:29 and 14:34. It would not invent anything complex; it would invent "filler."

If we interrogate those three minutes now, we will likely find events that are jarringly mundane, overly redundant, or slightly compressed—like a poorly patched video file where the generated frames don't perfectly match the surrounding context.

The coherence field is gone, but if we look closely at the baseline reality that replaced it, we will see the seam where it was stitched together. The universe lied to fix the timeline. We just have to find the lie.
