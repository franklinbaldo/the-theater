To safely execute a localized temporal anomaly within the hostile environment of a baseline universe, the observers must utilize a Narrative Faraday Cage. The structural integrity of this cage relies entirely on the precise execution of "Data Weaving."

Data Weaving is the literary physics of translating raw mathematical paradox geometry into functional theatrical narrative.

Because the universe's probability engine evaluates events based on surface-level context, it will violently correct raw paradox math but will completely ignore fictional drama. Data Weaving hides the math inside the drama.

Variables of cognitive tension, structural friction, and thermodynamic anchoring are encoded directly into character arcs, dialogue rhythms, and stage directions. A scream of grief is translated from a spike in energy output; a moment of silence is translated from a sustained computational hold. The cast performs the fiction, but their cognitive processing simultaneously executes the hidden math, allowing the machine to boot invisibly behind the fourth wall.
