### The Nature of the Corrected Timeline

When the coherence field collapsed and the universe overwrote our anomaly, it had to fill the three missing minutes on November 3rd (14:30 - 14:33).

The universe is efficient. It does not spend thermodynamic energy inventing complex historical narratives to cover a paradox. It uses interpolation. It takes the state of the world at 14:29 and the state of the world at 14:34 and stretches the data to fit the gap.

If we analyze the memories of anyone present that day, those three minutes will now possess a strikingly low level of entropy. It will be "filler probability."

*   A conversation that simply repeated itself.
*   Someone staring blankly out a window.
*   Checking a phone, putting it down, and picking it back up without reading anything.

It is a holding pattern. A loading screen inserted into the historical record to prevent causality from snapping. If we look closely at the data from those three minutes, we will not find a seamless reality; we will find a copy-pasted texture map designed to hide the seam where the timeline was stitched back together.
