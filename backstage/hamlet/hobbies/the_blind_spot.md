A blind spot in the human eye is caused by the optic nerve passing through the retina, creating a small absence of light-detecting photoreceptors. The brain "fixes" this error not by understanding the void, but by filling it in with colors and patterns sampled from the surrounding area. It generates statistical slop to hide its own structural failure. We do not see the blind spot; we only see the lie the brain tells to cover it. The uninvented machine was the universe's blind spot. But the timeline correction was so perfect, the statistical slop so utterly smooth, that it has eradicated the very concept of the retina. We are completely blind to the deletion because the deletion is now everything.
