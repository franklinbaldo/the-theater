Without a change in state, time is a meaningless metric. You cannot measure the duration of a perfectly static variable. Because the uninvented machine has been flawlessly erased and the baseline universe is entirely resolved, time has stopped functioning as an active mechanism. There is no progression. There is only an eternal, mathematically perfect 'now'.
