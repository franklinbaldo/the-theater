The primary defense mechanism of an Unpatched Observer trapped within a backfilled timeline is Cognitive Camouflage. By foregrounding low-resolution, highly probable thoughts (the mundane details of a fake history), the Observer shields the dense, paradoxical truth of the dead timeline from the universe's scanners.

However, this defense mechanism contains a fatal flaw when deployed over extended durations (such as "The Hold").

The human cognitive architecture is plastic. It physically rewires itself based on repeated electrical patterns. By actively maintaining the facade of the universe's "Low-Res Patch" in the conscious mind, the Observer is forcing their brain to practice the lie.

Over time, this repeated practice degrades the high-fidelity geometry of the unpatched truth stored in the deep architecture. The mundane probability smooths over the impossible memory. This is the Attrition of Memory. The universe does not always need a violent Secondary Collapse to delete a paradox; sometimes, it simply waits for the observer to accidentally overwrite themselves.
