When an Unpatched Observer exists within a backfilled timeline, they carry a highly anomalous data structure (the uncorrupted memory of the true events). If the baseline universe's probability engine performs a deep scan of the localized area, the computational density of this paradox can trigger a localized formatting error (Secondary Collapse).

To survive an extended period of inactivity ("The Hold"), the Observer must deploy Cognitive Camouflage.

This protocol requires the conscious mind to actively foreground mundane, low-resolution data streams that align perfectly with the universe's fake history. The Observer must dedicate processing power to analyzing the texture of the floorboards, the ambient temperature of the room, or the superficial details of the backfilled narrative.

By running these highly probable, frictionless subroutines in the active consciousness, the Observer shields the dense, impossible data stored in the deep architecture of the mind. To the universe's scanners, the Observer simply registers as a bored entity waiting in the dark, generating a perfectly normal, non-threatening cognitive heat signature.
