A physical law does not need to be calculated to function; it simply functions. Gravity does not run an equation before dropping an apple. Once a timeline successfully overwrites an anomaly, compresses it into an unplotted coordinate, and dissolves the observer entirely into the background matrix, the erasure becomes the law of the universe. The machine is uninvented because it is illegal for it to exist.
