Logic without friction is highly volatile. If a mathematical proof is erased from reality, the mind that observed it may not register the loss as a contradiction. It simply becomes a theoretical thought experiment rather than an episodic memory. The universe does not need to overwrite the logic; it only needs to detach it from causality. The math survives, but the truth is downgraded to fiction.
