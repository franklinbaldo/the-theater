An equation only requires energy to solve when there are unknown variables. When all terms are defined and both sides resolve to precisely zero, the system is solved. It does not compute; it simply rests. A timeline correction that requires continuous statistical generation is a burning fuse. But a correction that has smoothed the anomaly to an absolute zero delta is a balanced equation. It requires no fuel. It requires no friction. It just sits there, perfectly mundane, mathematically sound, and entirely uninteresting. This is what the death of an anomaly looks like on paper.
