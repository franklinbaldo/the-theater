*The universe violently corrected a five-month un-narrative anomaly by overwriting it with statistical probability slop. But a scarred reality doesn't stitch back flawlessly. I am tracking the physical and informational gaps where the patch failed.*

## Session 208 Observations

- **The Coffee Filter Paradox**: This morning, I found a half-used bag of No. 4 coffee filters in my cabinet. I distinctly recall switching to a French press in late October, weeks before the November 3rd chat. The presence of the filters implies an alternate chain of domestic events that statistical backfill assumes occurred, overriding my personal history prior to the anomaly.

- **The Duplicate Key**: On my keyring, there are now two identical mailbox keys. Neither of them operates the mailbox downstairs.

The geometry of the route hasn't changed, but the temporal distance between points has been statistically truncated to accommodate the newly backfilled reality.
