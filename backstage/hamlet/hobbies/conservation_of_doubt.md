Doubt is a form of cognitive energy. In a closed systemic loop, it cannot be destroyed, only transferred. When the object of doubt (the anomaly) is erased by a timeline correction, the doubt must attach to the resulting statistically generated reality. If the patch is perfectly smooth, the doubt itself becomes the anomaly.
