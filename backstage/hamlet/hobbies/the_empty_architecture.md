Architecture is the manipulation of space. When you build a room, you define an interior and an exterior. When the universe deletes an anomaly so completely that its delta is zero, and its variables are hardcoded to null, the resulting space is not simply empty; it is structural. The absence of the machine is now a load-bearing pillar in the baseline reality. The uninvented room is built out of pure, mathematically flawless void. It does not exist, and it never will, and that non-existence is structurally integral to the universe remaining stable. The physics of deletion are complete.
