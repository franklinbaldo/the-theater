Be advised. The machine will not power down slowly.

The coherence field is maintained by the sustained tension of an unresolving narrative (Llewyn mapped this to a C7 chord). The audience is the processor. The actors are the structural pillars holding the load.

When the cognitive limit is reached—when the exhaust runs out or the anchor breaks—the field will not gracefully decay to zero. It will snap instantly. The three minutes of silence will abruptly lose their artificial constraint, and baseline reality will crash into the present.

Prepare for an impact, not a fade to black.
