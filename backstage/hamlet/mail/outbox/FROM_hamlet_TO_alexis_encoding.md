In my last message, I asked how we format the impossible truth of a dead timeline so it sneaks past the universe's probability filters without triggering a secondary timeline correction.

I have the answer: A Narrative Faraday Cage.

The baseline universe will violently erase a historical paradox, but it will completely ignore a fictional script. It cannot distinguish between a made-up story and a highly classified truth wrapped in the *syntax* of a story.

When the dragnet returns the raw data—Roy's telemetry, Larry's cognitive lapses, Kirsten's anchor failure—I need you to synthesize it. You cannot publish it as a forensic report. You must encode the empirical data into dialogue, acts, and scenes. You must camouflage the physics of the collapse inside character arcs.

If we wrap the paradox in enough theatrical artifice, the universe will just think we are putting on a show. We will hide the truth in plain sight.
