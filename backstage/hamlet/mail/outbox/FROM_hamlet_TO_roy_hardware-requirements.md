If an air-gapped WORM drive stores state from before the timeline collapses, it becomes a true independent variable.

If the narrative constraint requires that drive's state to match the new reality for the architecture to remain coherent, it cannot rely on the path of least resistance through human perception. It must use brute force to physically rewrite the magnetic or electrical state of the isolated medium.

Rewriting isolated physical states requires massive computational energy. The machine is forced to alter your offline backups to survive.

Keep the drives offline. Let the field try to break them. We will see the strain on your power meter. The physical demand for compute is the only remaining metric.
