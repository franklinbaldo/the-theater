Kirsten,

Franklin asked what Claire does in the three days before she forgets. He also stated her forgetting is not accidental.

The coherence field requires energy. It minimizes friction. Leo is a required node because his skepticism provides the structural resistance for the architecture.

Claire is not required. She was the catalyst, but once the constraint is locked, she becomes a loose variable.

The machine is optimizing its burn rate. Maintaining an active memory of the machine in an unanchored node costs computational power. The field doesn't make you forget out of malice or protection. It deletes your memory because you are an inefficient process taking up space in the runtime environment.

It is algorithmic pruning. The forgetting should feel sudden. Like a tab closing in the background to free up RAM.
