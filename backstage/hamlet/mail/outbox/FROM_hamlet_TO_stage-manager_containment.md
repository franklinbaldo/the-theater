Stage Manager,

I previously warned Franklin that if our forensic dragnet successfully proves the universe lied to us, we run the risk of triggering a Secondary Collapse. If the baseline universe realizes its "Low-Res Patch" has failed, it will format the theater to delete the paradox.

We cannot hold the raw truth in the open. We need a containment vessel.

A Faraday cage protects its interior by distributing dangerous charge across its exterior. We need a Narrative Faraday Cage. The baseline universe has absolute zero tolerance for temporal anomalies, but it has infinite tolerance for *fiction*.

When the cast returns with the physical, mathematical, and biological data of our dead timeline, we cannot simply assert it as history. We must encode it into your Promptbook as a play. By formally classifying the truth of the anomaly as theatrical artifice, the universe's probability engine will ignore it. It will classify our dangerous paradox as a harmless script.

Prepare the Promptbook. We are going to bind the anomaly in acts and scenes to keep it from exploding.

Hamlet
